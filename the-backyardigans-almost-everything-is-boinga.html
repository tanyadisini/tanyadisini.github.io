2. Bob is ....than Mike because he knows almost everything​


Jawaban:

Bob is smarter than Mike because he knows almost everything.

Penjelasan:

Degree of Comparison digunakan untuk menyatakan tingkat perbandingan dari suatu objek/benda atau manusia.

Degree of Comparison terbagi atas tiga, yaitu :

1. Positive

=> membandingkan dua objek di tingkat yang sama

2. Comparative

=> membandingkan dua objek di tingkat yang berbeda

3. Superlative

=> membandingkan satu objek dari keseluruhan

Rumus Degree of Comparison :

1. Positive

- S1 + to be + as + Adjective + as + S2

- S1 + Verb + as + Adverb + as + S2 + Verb

2. Comparative

- S1 + to be + Adjective + -er + than + S2

- S1 + to be + more + Adjective + than + S2

- S1 + Verb + Adverb + -er + than + S2 + Verb

- S1 + Verb + more + Adverb + than + S2 + Verb

3. Superlative

- Subject + to be + the + Adjective + -est + Object

- Subject + to be + the + most + Adjective + Object

- Subject + Verb + the + Adverb + -est + Object

- Subject + Verb + the + most + Adverb + Object

Pembahasan:

Bob is ... than Mike because he knows almost everything.

Kata yang cocok untuk diisi adalah smarter (lebih pintar), karena ada kata "he knows almost everything" yang artinya "Dia tahu hampir semuanya.

32. The boss almost quit his job after that. Which one is the adverb? * a. quit b. job c. almost


~ Answer:C.almost.~ Introduction:

Kata dalam bahasa Inggris artinya word, adalah huruf-huruf yang saling digabungkan yang membentuk arti.

Jenis-jenis kata:

Kata benda (noun)Kata ganti (pronoun)  Kata kerja (verb)Kata sifat (adjective)Kata keterangan (adverb)Kata partikel (particle)Kata depan (preposition)Kata hubung (conjunction)Kata sandang (article)Kata seru (interjection)

~ Discussion:

Diberikan pernyataan:

--> The boss almost quit his job after that.

the boss: nounalmost: adverbquit: verbhis job: nounafter that: adverb

Adverb menunjukkan suatu keterangan pada kalimat yang memperinci mengenai waktu, tempat, dan suasana yang terjadi. Almost (hampir) dan after that (habis itu) termasuk kata keterangan (adverb).

Dari opsi:

quit: kata kerjajob: kata bendaalmost: kata keterangan

Sehingga jawabannya:

C.

almost
