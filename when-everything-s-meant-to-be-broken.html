I think..
It's used to say that it's okay and acceptable to not obey a rule.

But.. It could also mean that rules are meant to be broken when the circumstances call for it. 
For example: When we're in the library and there's a rule to be quiet. And then, there is a fire in another building, should we keep quiet or shout "fire!"?

There are times when we should think for ourselves and not obey every rule blindly.

So, no rules are meant to be followed all the time, there are exceptions to every rule.
