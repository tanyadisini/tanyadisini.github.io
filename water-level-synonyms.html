21. The pH level of bottled water is averaged at 6.9 with variance of 0.3, what is the probability of: a)Getting a bottled water with pH level less than 6.5?


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

[tex]\sigma^2 = 0.3 \to \sigma = 0.548, \mu = 6.9[/tex]

a. X = 6.5 => P(X < 6.5) = ?

[tex]Z = \dfrac{\overline{X}-\mu}{\sigma}\\\\Z = \dfrac{6.5-6.9}{0.548} = -0.73\\\\P(\overline X< 6.5) = P(Z < -0.73)\\\\\text{berdasarkan tabel Z :}\\\\P(Z < -0.73) = 0.2327\\\\\boxed{\boxed{\textbf{\huge{$\boldsymbol{P(\overline X< 6.5) = 0.2327 = 23.27\%}$}}}}[/tex]

33. during the flash flood, the water level of a river rises by 10cm every hour. Five hours later,the water level starts to subside by 4 cm every half-hour.If the water level before the flash flood is 12m , determine the water, in m , level of the river 8 hours after the flood.


The answer is , 11,86. Before the flood , the water level is 12m. After 5 hours , the water increases about 0,50m. So, the water level after 5 hours is 12,50m. And then, the water level starts to subside by 4cm ever half-hour. So, after 8 hours, the water level is 11,86. How? 12,50m , the water level after flood, and then, subtract it with 64 cm, which is 0,64 m so, the result is 11,86. Hope this is correct.
