20. Answer the following questions correctly! 1. What do you know about marketing? 2. Where do you experience marketing in everyday life? 3. What is the function of marketing? 4. Mention and explain three roles of marketing! 5. What are the differences among marketing, advertising, and sales? 6. What is the marketing concept? How does it differ from the production concept, the product concept, or the selling concept? 7. Are customers and consumers different? Why? 8. What is the product and brand? 9. What are the differences between product and brand? 10. Mention 7p's or 4p's of marketing mix!​


Jawaban:

1. Marketing is the process of promoting and selling products or services to customers. It involves understanding customer needs, identifying target markets, creating and communicating value propositions, and managing customer relationships to achieve organizational goals.

2. Marketing is experienced in everyday life through various channels such as television advertisements, social media promotions, email marketing, billboards, product packaging, and even word-of-mouth recommendations from friends or family.

3. The main function of marketing is to satisfy customer needs and wants profitably. It involves creating, communicating, and delivering value to customers, while also building strong relationships to retain them and gain a competitive advantage in the market.

4. Three roles of marketing are:

  a. Market Research: Gathering and analyzing data about customers, competitors, and market trends to understand consumer preferences and make informed business decisions.

  b. Product Development and Innovation: Creating and improving products or services to meet customer demands and stay ahead in the market.

  c. Promotion and Communication: Creating effective marketing campaigns to reach target audiences, raise awareness about products, and persuade customers to make a purchase.

5. Marketing involves the overall process of identifying customer needs, creating products or services to meet those needs, promoting and communicating the offerings, and selling them to customers. Advertising is a part of marketing and refers to the specific process of promoting products or services through various media channels. Sales, on the other hand, is the direct interaction between the seller and the customer to close the deal.

6. The marketing concept focuses on understanding and meeting customer needs and wants. It differs from other concepts like the production concept (prioritizing efficient production), the product concept (focusing on product quality and features), and the selling concept (emphasizing aggressive sales tactics) because it puts customer satisfaction at the center of all marketing activities.

7. Yes, customers and consumers are different. Customers are individuals or entities that buy products or services, while consumers are the end-users or individuals who actually use or consume the products or services.

8. A product is a tangible item or intangible service offered to satisfy customer needs or wants. A brand, on the other hand, is the identity, image, or reputation associated with a specific product or company in the minds of customers.

9. The main difference between a product and a brand is that a product is a physical item or service, while a brand is the emotional and psychological associations, values, and perceptions that customers attach to that product. Brands often help differentiate products in a crowded market and can influence customer loyalty and purchasing decisions.

10. The 7Ps (Marketing Mix) are:

  a. Product: The offering or service provided to customers.

  b. Price: The amount customers pay for the product or service.

  c. Place: The distribution channels and locations where the product is available.

  d. Promotion: The marketing activities used to communicate and promote the product.

  e. People: The people involved in delivering the product or service, including employees and customer service representatives.

  f. Process: The processes and systems involved in delivering the product or service.

  g. Physical Evidence: The physical elements that represent the product or service, such as packaging or the store environment.

The 4Ps are a subset of the 7Ps and include Product, Price, Place, and Promotion.

22. Apa yg di maksud marketing & promotion lima kunci sukses berjualan di e-commerce


Jawaban:

lima Kunci Sukses Berjualan di e Commerce berikut merupakan cara -cara yang paling tepat untuk mulai berbinis di e commerce.

1. Pelayanan yang Baik

Point utama kunci sukses berjualan di e commerce adalah pelayanan yang baik. Berikan yang terbaik untuk konsumen anda.

Konsumen anda akan datang lagi, lagi dan lagi jika pelayanan yang anda berikan dapat memuaskan kebutuhan mereka.

2. Cepat Respon

Beriktunya yang menjadi Kunci Sukses Berjualan di e Commerce adalah kecepatan Respon anda. Mungkin anda juga akan kesal jika anda ingin bertanya tentang sebuah produk yang anda inginkan namun tidak direspon penjual.

Nah begitupun dengan konsumen e commerce anda. Pelanggan anda akan sangat suka jika anda bisa cepat respon dalam menaggapi pertanyaa mereka. Jadi untuk itu, buatlah Bisnis e commerce anda menjadi bisnis yang banyak di sukai customer anda dengan pelayanan respon cepat.

3. Berikan Diskon dan Penawaran yang Menarik

Orang akan membeli sebuah produk jika yang di tawarkan ke mereka menarik. Salah satu strategi penjualan produk yang tepat adalah penawaran yang menarik.

Anda akan mendapatkan banyak interaksi pelanggan jika anda mampu memberikan penawaran yang tepat. Selain itu anda bisa mengiklankan produk anda untuk meluaskan penawaran produk anda.

Sesekali berikan Diskon ke pelanggan setia anda.Atau berikan promosi jika mereka membeli item tertentu atau dengan kuantiti tertentu. Ini akan menjadi ciri khas dan keunggulan dari bisnis e commerce anda.

4. Harga yang Kompetitif

Berjualan di e commerce tidaklah mudah. terdapat ribuan penjual di internet dan semua adalah pesaing anda dalam Bisnis e commerce. Lalu bagaimana agar anda dapat bersaing dengan mereka?.

Caranya adalah membuat harga yang kompetitif, jangan menjual produk yang sama dengan harga yang lebih dari kompetitor anda. Maka anda mampu bersaing denga mereka.

5. Menawarkan Berbagai Produk yang berkualitas

Kunci Sukses Berjualan di e Commerce terakhir adalah dengan memberikan Produk yang berkualitas. Orang mencari produk yang mereka inginkan dengan kualitas yang baik.

1. Advertising (Periklanan): Elemen ini melibatkan penggunaan media seperti iklan televisi, iklan cetak, iklan digital, atau iklan radio untuk mempromosikan produk kepada target audiens. Contoh: Pembuatan iklan televisi yang kreatif untuk sebuah merek makanan.

2. Promotion (Promosi): Promosi melibatkan aktivitas yang dirancang untuk meningkatkan kesadaran dan minat pelanggan terhadap produk. Contoh: Menawarkan diskon khusus atau mengadakan acara penjualan untuk menarik minat pelanggan.

3. Publicity (Pengarahan): Pengarahan melibatkan pemberitaan atau sorotan media yang tidak dibayar untuk mempromosikan produk secara alami. Contoh: Produk yang mendapatkan eksposur positif melalui artikel berita yang menyoroti manfaatnya.

4. Public Relations (Hubungan Publik): Hubungan publik melibatkan upaya membangun citra positif merek dan hubungan yang baik dengan stakeholder seperti media, konsumen, dan masyarakat umum. Contoh: Melibatkan perwakilan merek dalam kegiatan amal atau menyebarkan berita positif kepada media.

5. Sales (Penjualan): Penjualan melibatkan aktivitas menjual produk langsung kepada pelanggan. Contoh: Interaksi penjualan langsung melalui toko fisik atau melalui platform e-commerce.

6. Marketing (Pemasaran): Pemasaran melibatkan serangkaian strategi dan taktik yang digunakan untuk memenuhi kebutuhan dan keinginan pelanggan, termasuk mengidentifikasi target pasar, pengembangan produk, penetapan harga, dan penempatan produk. Contoh: Mengidentifikasi segmentasi pasar yang tepat untuk produk dan merancang kampanye pemasaran yang efektif.

Harap diingat bahwa contoh-contoh ini bersifat umum dan dapat bervariasi tergantung pada produk yang ingin Anda identifikasi.

32. Tolong di jawab dgn benar yh. Apa perbedaan marketing, advertising, press agentry dan propaganda dan berikan contohnya.


Answers:

Marketing - Marketing refers to activities a company undertakes to promote the buying or selling of a product or service. Marketing includes advertising, selling, and delivering products to consumers or other businesses.

examples: branding, showrooms, customary pricing, direct marketing etc.

Advertising - Advertising is a marketing tactic involving paying for space to promote a product, service, or cause. The actual promotional messages are called advertisements, or ads for short. The goal of advertising is to reach people most likely to be willing to pay for a company's products or services and entice them to buy.

examples: newspapers, magazines, websites, brochures etc

Press Agentry - Press agentry is the practice of attracting the attention of the press through technique that manufactures news. Methods associated with press agentry include staged events, publicity stunts, faux rallies or gatherings, spinning, and hype.

examples: press agentry model, public information model, two-way asymmetrical model, and two-way symmetrical model

Propaganda - Propaganda is communication that is primarily used to influence an audience and further an agenda, which may not be objective and may be selectively presenting facts in order to encourage a particular synthesis or perception, or using loaded language in order to produce an emotional rather than a rational response to the information that is being presented.

examples: testimonials, bandwagon, transfer, logical fallacies, etc
