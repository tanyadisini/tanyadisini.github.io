Jawaban:

In the first verse, the songwriter tries to awaken the unconscious that every human being has a heart. In addition, songwriters also believe that in every human heart, there is love. By inserting the words "heart" and "love" in the lyrics, songwriters seem to be able to vibrate the hearts of their listeners. We know that the heart is a symbol of one's sincerity, because in the heart there is a love and a feeling of affection.

Thus, if someone already has feelings of love and affection in his heart, someone will definitely be a good person, who can help others, and make this world a better and brighter future.

Thus, if someone already has feelings of love and affection in his heart, someone will definitely be a good person, who can help others, and make this world a better and brighter future.

In the chorus / reff line, the songwriter reiterates his hopes, so that the world in the future will change to a better world. in the lyrics of this song the song writer also seems to say that the world we live in is already very cruel, many people are dying to death because of possible hunger, war, and so on. However, many people do not care about this condition. Therefore, songwriters invite us to bring the world back to normal, inviting us to make the world more peaceful with all the love in our hearts. A peaceful world will bring extraordinary happiness in life. Not only for others, but also for ourselves.

23. Analyse the ing and find the messeges byond the lyrics dari lagu let it go


Answer:

The song 'Let it go', which is the original soundtrack of the phenomenal animated movie, 'Frozen' has been popular among children, young adult and adult. Here are some -ing words in the lyric. 
- howling (as a verb for present continous)
- swirling (gerund)
- spiraling (as a verb for present continous)
- going (same as above)
The first three Ving is a quite descriptive word. 

The message beyond the song is that you need to embrace who you are along with your potential. Never mind about others say that you are not ideal or perfect as others see, but ideal in your own way. Such can be seen visually to get the meaning actually if you see the movie. This song was played in the full length for the first time at the scene where the older sister has been aware of her power to be the ice queen and start embracing herself as well as controlling it instead of being fear about it.

Mr. Jackson is an American teacher, but he lives and works in Denpasar. He teaches English at SMP 8. He is very a good teacher. His students like him. His wife is an Indonesian. She comes from Medan. The Jackson's have a house maid to help Mrs Jackson to do the work. The maid comes from Bantul.
