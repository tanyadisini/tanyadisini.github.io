If I live in a place without nobody supporting me I will find a way to survive on my own using the self survival knowledge I have learned from the internet.

First of all, I will try to find a safe location that has water nearby because water is the most important substance to stay alive.

Secondly, I will build a safe shelter to sleep in and make hunting tools so I can catch animals for food and make clothes to protect from the cold.

Thirdly, I can make fire by rubbing 2 pieces of woods until they are hot resulted in some sparks to make fire. With this fire I can cook the animals I have caught.

Lastly, I will hope that somebody will come along and company me so we can build a community to help each other.

Pembahasan:

Teks di atas adalah termasuk kategori Descriptive Text dimana penulis mencoba menceritakan tentang sebuah kejadian. Descriptive Text adalah sebuah text yang menjelaskan tentang sesuatu benda, seseorang, suatu kejadian. Suatu lokasi, dan sebagainya dimana penulis mencoba untuk memberi gambaran yang jelas kepada pembaca dengan detail-detail dan rincian dari hal yang diceritakan tersebut. Dengan demikian pembaca akan memahami penuh apa yang ia baca.

Descriptive Text dibagi menjadi 2 bagian:

Bagian Identification – Bagian ini umumnya terdapat pada awal paragraph dimana penulis mencoba memberikan gambaran garis besar mengenai apa yang akan diceritakan selanjutnya sehingga mempermudah pemahaman alur cerita bagi pembaca.   Bagian Description – Bagian ini mengandung detail dan rincian penjelasan tentang hal-hal yang ingin diceritakan oleh penulis sehingga pembaca memiliki gambaran dan pemahaman yang menyeluruh tentang apa yang dituliskan oleh penulis.

Ciri-ciri dari Descriptive Text:

Pada umumnya dituliskan dalam bentuk Simple Present Tense.Menggunakan banyak kata sifat (adjective).Banyak menggunakan kata kerja penghubung (relating verb) seperti is/am/are, has/have, dan lain-lain.

17. Siti,where(you,be) before you (go)to school this morning?i (call) you around six to remind you to bring my dictionary to school. But,nobody (pick) up the phone?


SIMPLE PAST TENSE

Answer :

Siti, where were you before you went to school this morning? I called you around six to remind you to bring my dictionary to school. But, nobody picked up the phone?

Explanation :

Simple past tense mendeskripsikan tindakan yang telah dimulai dan diselesaikan juga pada waktu tertentu di masa lampau . Durasi waktu kegiatan tidak ditentukan, bisa saja baru - baru ini telah terjadi dan kejadian yang memang sudah lama terjadi di masa lampau.

Berdasarkan struktur kalimatnya, dibagi 2 jenis yaitu verbal sentence & Nominal sentence .

1. Verbal sentence

merupakan struktur kalimat yang predikatnya berupa kata kerja. Contoh : eat, run, drink, do, walk, read, watch, swim and etc.

Pattern of Verbal Sentence

(+) Subject + Verb 2 + Object/Complement  

(-) Subject + did + not + verb 1 + Object/ Complement

(?) Did+Subject+Verb 1 + Object/Complement ?

2.Nominal sentence

memiliki struktur kalimat dimana predikatnya bukan berupa kata kerja. Pada umumnya diikuti kata benda (noun), kata sifat (adjective) dan keterangan ( adverb ).

Pattern of nominal sentence :

(+) Subject + tobe ( was/were ) + noun/adjective/adverb

(-) Subject + tobe(was/were) + not + noun/adjective/adverb  

(?) tobe(was/were)+Subject + noun/adjective/adverb?

ADVERB OF TIME

( Keterangan waktu identifikasi bentuk simple past)

1. Definite point in time ( waktu penentu jelas )

→ last week, yesterday, two years ago, last night, this morning

Example :

Verbal

(+ ) She  met her friend this morning.

( - ) She did not meet her friend this morning.

( ? ) Did she meet her friend this morning?

Nominal

(+ ) They were in Yogyakarta last year.

( - )  They were not in Yogyakarta last year.

(? ) Were They in Yogyakarta last year  ?

2. An indefinite point in time ( dalam waktu yang tidak ditentukan )

→ a long time ago, the other day

Example :

Verbal  

(+ ) He lived with his grandparents  when he was young.    

(- )  He did not live with his grandparents  when he was young.

( ? ) Did he live with his grandparents when he was young?

Nominal

(+) He was an army  a long time ago      

(- ) He wasn't an army a  long time ago

( ? ) Was he an army a long time ago ?

PERTANYAAN WH-WORD DALAM SIMPLE PAST TENSE :

RUMUS :

Nominal

WH-Word +was/were+subject+ Complement?

Example :

Where were you couple days ago?
