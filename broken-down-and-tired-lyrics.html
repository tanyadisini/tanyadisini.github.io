6. what are the matirials that cannot be broken down by a decomposer​


Jawaban:

Non-biodegradable substances

Penjelasan:

Non-biodegradable substances are those chemical substances which are not broken down by the biological means (decomposers). They persist in the environment for a long time, as they are not degraded by the decomposers and cause harm to the members of the ecosystem by causing biological magnification. Non-biodegradable chemicals like fertilizers, pesticides affect the fertility of soil which reduces the crop yield. Whereas, biodegradable substances are broken down by the decomposers because they possess the specific enzymes for their degradation.

37. Find 2 lyrics of the song that use Present Perfect Tense and 5 lyrics that use Simple Past Tense!​


Jawaban:

Kalimat "Find 2 lyrics of the song that use Present Perfect Tense and 5 lyrics that use Simple Past Tense!" berarti "Temukan 2 lirik lagu yang menggunakan Present Perfect Tense dan 5 lirik yang menggunakan Simple Past Tense!"

Kalian dapat mencari lirik lagu yang dimaksud menggunakan pola kalimat berikut sebagai acuan:

1.Present Perfect Tense

(+) S + have/has + Past participle atau S + has/have + been + O

(-) S + have/has not + Past participle atau S + has/have not + been + O

(?) Have/has + S + Past participle? atau has/have + S + been + O?

2.Simple Past Tense

(+) S + Verb 2 + O atau S + to be (was/were) + O

(-) S + did not + Verb 1 atau S + to be (was/were) not + O

(?) Did + S + Verb 1 atau Was/Were + S + . . . ?

Salah satu contoh lirik lagu tersebut adalah:

Present Perfect Tense

"Or have you completely erased me from your memory?", lirik lagu Don't You Remember - Adele.

Simple Past Tense

"You gave me wings and made me fly", lirik lagu Because you love me - Celine Dion.
