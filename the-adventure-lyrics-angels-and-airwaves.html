Orientation, Complication and Resolution of Jaka Tarub and 7 Angels:

Orientation: Once upon a time there was a widow who lived in the village of Dadapan. She had a son whose name was Joko Tarup. Dadapan village was close to a wood so Joko Tarup liked to go to the wood. He liked hunting for animals with his blowpipe.

Complication 1: Jaka Tarub steal one of angel's Dress.

Resolution 1: Nawang Wulan (The angels) became Jaka Tarub's wife. They had a daughter named Nawang Sih.

Complication 2: Nawang wulang has a spiritual power to cook only with one rice. She asked her husband not to open the bowl before it was done.

Resolution 2: Jaka Tarub broke his promise and open the bowl.

Complication 3: Nawang wulan cook manually. The stock of rice started to diminished. Nawang Wulan found that her dress was hidden under the piles of rice.

Resolution 3: Nawang Wulan flew to heaven and left Jaka Tarub and their daughter.

Penjelasan:

Narrative Text

Narrative text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menghibur pembacanya. Terdapat beberapa jenis narrative text, yaitu:

1. Fable

2. Legend

3. Fairy tale

4. Horror

5. Romance

6. Mystery

7. Myth

8. dan lain lain

Generic Structure dari Narrative Text adalah:

• Orientation : It is about the opening paragraph where the characters of the story are introduced.(berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat dan waktu terjadinya cerita (siapa atau apa, kapan dan dimana)

• Complication : Where the problems in the story developed. (Permasalahan muncul / mulai terjadi dan berkembang)

• Resolution : Where the problems in the story is solved. Masalah selesai, secara baik “happy ending” ataupun buruk “bad ending”.

• Coda / reorientation (optional) – lesson from the story

Language Features dari Narrative Text adalah:

• Use Past tense (killed, drunk, etc)

• Use adverb of time (Once upun a time, one day, etc)

• Use time conjunction (when, then, suddenly, etc)

• Use specific character. The character of the story is specific, not general. (Cinderella, Snow White, Alibaba, etc)

• Use action verbs. A verb that shows an action. (killed, dug, walked, etc)

• Use direct speech. It is to make the story lively. (Snow White said,”My name is Snow White). The direct speech uses present tense.

Kalimat "Find 2 lyrics of the song that use Present Perfect Tense and 5 lyrics that use Simple Past Tense!" berarti "Temukan 2 lirik lagu yang menggunakan Present Perfect Tense dan 5 lirik yang menggunakan Simple Past Tense!"

Kalian dapat mencari lirik lagu yang dimaksud menggunakan pola kalimat berikut sebagai acuan:

1.Present Perfect Tense

(+) S + have/has + Past participle atau S + has/have + been + O

(-) S + have/has not + Past participle atau S + has/have not + been + O

(?) Have/has + S + Past participle? atau has/have + S + been + O?

2.Simple Past Tense

(+) S + Verb 2 + O atau S + to be (was/were) + O

(-) S + did not + Verb 1 atau S + to be (was/were) not + O

(?) Did + S + Verb 1 atau Was/Were + S + . . . ?

Salah satu contoh lirik lagu tersebut adalah:

Present Perfect Tense

"Or have you completely erased me from your memory?", lirik lagu Don't You Remember - Adele.

Simple Past Tense

"You gave me wings and made me fly", lirik lagu Because you love me - Celine Dion.
