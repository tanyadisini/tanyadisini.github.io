29. 2 • At the pet shop, the kittens are fed every 3 hours and the baby fishes are fed every 2 hours. At what time will both be fed at the same time if the first fed at 9:00a.m.? ​


Jawaban:

15.00 p.m

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

the kittens = every 3 hours

the baby fishes = every 2 hours

find the least common multiple (LCM) of 3 and 2

multiples of 3 = 3, 6, 9, ....

multiples of 2 = 2, 4, 6, ....

LCM of 2 and 3 = 6

So if the first fed at 9:00 a.m , then both fed at the sama time = 9:00 + 6 hours = 15.00 p.m
