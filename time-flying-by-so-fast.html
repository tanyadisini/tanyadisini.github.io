33. Kirana..... (run) so fast because there (be) ai dog


Kirana ran (Verb 2 : Past, of run) so fast because there's a dog



Kalo salah maafin ya...
smga bermanfaatJawaban: Kirana ran so fast because there was a dog.


Penjelasan:

Disini kata yang digunakan berupa past tense. Past tense rumusnya kira kira seperti ini:

( subject + verb + ed + object)  atau ( subject + verb2 + object)

Contoh: I dropped a coin into the machine. I stopped the car with my skill. He moved the table into the bedroom. 

Namun ada juga kata yang tidak perlu ditambah "ed" yang memang sudah diatur dari sananya. 
Contoh: did, wrote, took, went.. 


Dan untuk keberadaan benda di masa lalu, kita menggunakan kata "was" atau "were" 
Contoh: disana ada pesta 2 hari yang lalu = There was a party two days ago.

Perlu diingat, "was" hanya dipakai untuk objek yang hanya satu. Untuk objek yang banyak, pakai kata "were" - > There were two cats walking around.

Selain itu untuk subjek subjek tertentu:
I -> was , contoh: I was there last night. (tidak perlu pakai "were")
You -> were
He -> was
She -> was
They -> were
It -> was
Those -> were
These -> were

Maaf jika ada kesalahan. Semoga membantu :)

Gambar 1

Rs=R₁ + R₂

=2 Ω + 3 Ω

= 5 Ω

I = V/R

= 10 v / 5 Ω

Gambar 2

R₁ = 2 Ω

R₂ = 3 Ω

V = 10 v

1/Rp=1/R₁ + 1/R₂

=1/2 Ω + 1/3 Ω

=3/6 Ω + 2/6 Ω

=5/6 Ω

Rp =6/5 Ω

=1 1/5 Ω

I = V/ R

= 10 V / (1 1/5 Ω)

= 10 / (6/5)

= 10 x (5/6)

= 50/6

= 8 2/6

= 8 1/3 A
