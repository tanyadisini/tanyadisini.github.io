Passive voice subject bertukar dengan object , begitu pula sebaliknya. subjek di Passive voice tidak melakukan tindakan, yg melakukan tindakan object.

Passive voice simple future tense

(+) Objek + will + be + Verb 3 + by + Subjek + complement

(-) Objek + won't + be + Verb 3 + by + Subjek + complement

(?) will + objek + be + verb3 + by + subject?

(-) Objek + be (was/were) not + Being + verb3 + by + S

(?) was/were + objek + being + verb3 + by subjek?

Passive voice présent Perfect tense

(+) Objek + Aux. (has/have) + been + verb 3 + by + S

(-) Objek + Aux. (has/have) not + been + verb 3 + by + S

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan menggunakan bentuk kalimat Passive Voice.

Rumus Passive Voice:

1. Passive Voice [Simple Present Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Verb3

2. Passive Voice [Present Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + being + Verb3

3. Passive Voice [Present Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + have/has + been + Verb3

4. Passive Voice [Simple Past Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Verb 3

5. Passive Voice [Past Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Being + Verb3

6. Passive Voice [Past Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + had + been + Verb3

7. Passive Voice [Simple Future Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will + be + Verb3

8. Passive Voice [Future Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will be + being + Verb3

9. Passive Voice [Future Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will have + been + Verb3

Change the active to the passive

1. two horses were pulling the farmer's wagon
⇒ the farmer's wagon was being pulled by two horses

2. Shakespeare wrote that play
⇒ that play was written by shakespeare

3. bob will invite Anna to the party tomorrow
⇒ anna will be invited by bob to the party tomorrow

4. Alex is preparing that report
⇒ that report is being prepared by ale

﻿Reading Comprehension:

1. where do you probably read the announcement?

﻿I probably read the announcement at a train station.

2. who is the announcement address to?

﻿The announcement is addressed to the train passengers.

3.what is the announcement about?

﻿The announcement is about the arrival of the train at Gambir railway station on track 1.

4. what should the passenger do?

The passengers should have their luggage before they leave the station and they also have to make sure that nothing is left behind.

﻿There are eight wagons and the last is a box car. The wagons are: the first is the loco for the engineer followed by the engine and the restoration wagon. Then wagon number one two three four and six follow the last is the boxcar.
