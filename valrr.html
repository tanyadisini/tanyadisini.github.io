Pembahasan

1. What issue does the author argue?

Answer: The author/writer brings up the issue of different cultures in Indonesian so that Indonesian people should value all the differences to promote the unity of the nation.

2. Why should Indonesian people raise the tolerance among them?

Answer: Indonesian people should raise tolerance among them to maintain unity among differences.

3. Why is it forbidden to make a judgment of comparative value of other cultures?

Answer: because every culture has different customs and beliefs

4. What is the benefit of a plurality of a nation?

Answer: The benefit of a plurality of the nation is for cultural development and cultural exchange, the cross-cultural understanding among cultures will make the world a better place and preserves the unity of the nation.

5. What recommendation does the author propose?

Answer: The author's recommendation is that it is important to teach the younger generations about the importance of cultural identity and nationalism to promote the unity of the nation.

9. aurel membeli 2 sepatu dan 3 sandal seharga Rp 175.000 , sedangkan valeri membeli 3 sepatu & 4 sandal dengn harga Rp 255.000 . Harga 1 sepatu dan 2 sandal adalah ... a. Rp 105.000 b. Rp 95.000 c. Rp 90.000 d. Rp 71 000


Penyelesaian :
Misalnya :
Sapatu = x
Sandal = y

Diketahui :
- aurel membeli 2 sepatu dan 3 sandal seharga Rp 175.000
=> 2x + 3y = Rp. 175.000
- valeri membeli 3 sepatu & 4 sandal dengn harga Rp 255.000
=> 3x + 4y = Rp. 255.000

Ditanya :
Harga 1 sepatu dan 2 sandal adalah ...
=> x + 2y = ?

Jawab :
=> Menentukan Harga sandal (y)
2x + 3y = Rp. 175.000 | × 3
3x + 4y = Rp. 255.000 | × 2
____________________
=
6x + 9y = Rp. 525.000
6x + 8y = Rp. 510.000
__________________-
y = Rp. 15.000

=> Menentukan Harga sepatu (x)
2x + 3y = Rp. 175.000
2x + 3(Rp. 15.000) = Rp. 175.000
2x + Rp. 45.000 = Rp. 175.000
2x = Rp. 175.000 - Rp. 45.000
x = Rp. 130.000 / 2
x = Rp. 65.000

=> Menentukan Harga 1 sepatu dan 2 sandal
x + 2y
= Rp. 65.000 + 2(Rp. 15.000)
= Rp. 65.000 + Rp. 30.000
= Rp. 95.000
Jadi, Harga 1 sepatu dan 2 sandal adalah Rp. 95.000 ✔
