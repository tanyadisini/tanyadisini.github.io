26. tell me everything, you know about the cavities the human speech all types of phonetic active passive articulator


Jawaban:

1) Voice

In English we have both voiced and voiceless sounds. A sound fits into one of these categories according to how the vocal folds behave when a speech sound is produced.

Voiced: Voiced sounds are sounds that involve vocal fold vibrations when they are produced. Examples of voiced sounds are /b,d,v,m/.

If you place two fingers on either side of the front of your neck, just below your jawbone, and produce a sound, you should be able to feel a vibrating sensation. This tells you that a sound is voiced.

Voiceless: Voiceless sounds are sounds that are produced with no vocal fold vibration. Examples of voiceless sounds in English are /s,t,p,f/.

2) Place

The vocal tract is made up of different sections, which play a pivotal role in the production of speech. These sections are called articulators and are what make speech sounds possible. They can be divided into two types.

The active articulator is the articulator that moves towards another articulator in the production of a speech sound. This articulator moves towards another articulator to form a closure of some type in the vocal tract (i.e open approximation, close, etc – define)

The passive articulator is the articulator that remains stationary in the production of a speech sound. Often, this is the destination that the active articulator moves towards (i.e the hard palate).

Jawaban:

Two of Everything

Orientation

Once upon a time, old and poor Mr. Haktak found a large brass pot in his garden. He decided to put his purse in it. Then, he carried it home. Mrs. Haktak was very happy with the pot.

Pada dahulu kala, Pak Haktak yang tua dan miskin menemukan sebuah belanga kuningan di kebunnya. Ia memutuskan untuk menempatkan tas kecilnya di dalam belanga tersebut. Kemudian, ia membawanya pulang. Bu Haktak sangat senang dengan belanga tersebut.

Complication

Suddenly, her hairpin fell into the pot. Mrs. Haktak reached and pulled out two hairpins and two purses. What a magic pot! Then they began to drop items into it and soon had two of everything.

One day, Mr. Haktak went to a market. At home, Mrs. Haktak did house works then rested for a while. She stooped over that pot to look inside. At that moment, Mr. Haktak kicked the door because his hands were full of packages. Bang! Mrs. Haktak lost her balance and fell into the pot. Mr. Haktak found that he had two of Mrs. Haktak. This presented a problem.

Tiba-tiba, jepit rambutnya jatuh ke dalam belanga tersebut. Bu Haktak meraih dan menarik dua jepit rambut dan dua tas kecil. Sungguh belanga yang ajaib! Kemudian mereka mulai menjatuhkan barang-barang kedalamnya dan tak lama kemudian mereka memiliki dua buah barang dari apapun yang mereka miliki.

Suatu hari, Pak Haktak pergi ke pasar. Di rumah, Bu Haktak mengerjakan pekerjaan rumah kemudian beristirahat sebentar. Ia berhenti di depan belanga untuk melihat isinya. Disaat itu juga, Pak Haktak menendang pintu rumah karena tangannya penuh dengan barang-barang bawaan. Bang! Bu Haktak kehilangan keseimbangannya dan jatuh ke dalam belanga. Pak Haktak menyadari dia mempunyai dua Bu Haktak. Hal ini menimbulkan masalah.
