12. Today I'm planning on wearing my .... shoes for the occasion. But I can't find them anywhere!A. formal brown leatherB. brown formal leatherC. brown leather formalD. leather formal brown


Urutan adjectives adalah :
D = Determiner (the, a, an, some, dll)
O = Opinion (beautiful, rich, diligent, dll)
S = Size (big, small, long, dll)
A = Age (young, old, new)
S = Shape (round, fat, thin, dll)
C = Color (white, yellow, red, dll)
O = Origin (Italy, Indonesia, Malaysia, dll)
M = Material (rubber, plastic, cotton, dll)
P = Purpose (tennis, school, dll)


Therefore the answer is
C. brown (C) leather (M) formal (P) shoesPelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
Materi : Adjective Order

Jawab :
A. Formal Brown Leather

Melihat urutan urutannya yaitu, Determiner-Opini-Ukuran-Usia-Bentuk-Warna-Asal usul-Material-Purpose + Noun

Formal termasuk dalam opini, brown termasuk dalam warna, leather termasuk dalam material
