17. 8. Make a short dialog of a job interview!


Jawaban:

Mike: Good Morning, John. I am Mike.

John: Good Morning.

Mike: How are you doing?

John: I am doing fine. Thank you.

Mike: How was the traffic coming over here?

John: I am so glad that the traffic was light this morning. No traffic jam and no accidents.

Mike: That is good. John, let’s start the interview. Are you ready?

John: Yes, I am.

Mike: First of all, let me properly introduce myself. I am the Finance Department Manager. As you know there is an open position in my department, and I need to fill this position as soon as possible.

John: Please, tell me a little bit about the position.

Mike: It is an entry-level position. The new employee will have to work closely with the  accounting department. He will also have to deal with the bank on a daily basis.

John: What type of qualifications do you require?

Mike: I require a four-year college degree in Finance. Some working experience would be helpful.

John: What kind of experience are you looking for?

Mike: Doing office work is good. However, since this is an entry-level position, I do not require a lot of experience. I am willing to train the new person.

John: That is great!

Mike: John, tell me a little bit about yourself.

John: I was a student at West Coast University, and I just graduated with a Bachelor degree in  Finance. I have been working part-time as a payroll clerk for the last two years.  

Mike: What are you looking for in a job?

John: The job should help me see what Finance is all about. I have learned a lot of Finance theories at school, and now it is time for me to put them into practice.

Mike: Anything else?

John: I also hope that it will help me grow in my field.

Mike: What are your strengths? Why should I hire you?

John: I am a hard-working person and a fast learner. I am very eager to learn, and I get along fine with people.

Mike: OK. Now, let me ask you a few quick questions. You do not mind working long hours, do you?

John: No, I do not.

Mike: Can you handle pressure?

John: Yes, I can. When I was going to school, I took quite a few courses each semester while working at least twenty hours every week. And, I handled that situation very well.

Mike: Do you still have any questions for me?

John: No, I think I have a pretty good understanding of the job. I believe that I can handle it with ease, and I hope to have the opportunity to work for you.

Mike: John, nice meeting you. Thank you for coming.

John: Nice meeting you too. Thank you for seeing me.

20. What is a job interview​


Jawaban:

A job interview is a formal meeting between a job candidate and a potential employer or hiring manager. The purpose of a job interview is to evaluate the candidate's qualifications, skills, and suitability for the job, and to assess whether the candidate would be a good fit for the company or organization. During a job interview, the employer may ask a series of questions to assess the candidate's knowledge, experience, and personality, and to determine whether the candidate has the necessary skills and abilities to perform the job successfully. A job interview can take place in person, over the phone, or via video conferencing, and may be conducted by one or more interviewers.

Here is an example of a job interview for a customer service representative position:

Interviewer: Can you tell us about your previous experience in customer service?

Candidate: Sure, I have worked in customer service for three years at a call center, handling customer inquiries and complaints. I have experience with both phone and email support.

Interviewer: What is the essential quality a customer service representative should possess?

Candidate: Excellent communication skills are the essential quality a customer service representative should possess. Listening actively to customers, understanding their concerns, and providing clear and empathetic responses are crucial.

Interviewer: How do you handle demanding customers?

Candidate: I always approach demanding customers with patience and empathy. I try to understand their concerns and find a solution to satisfy them. If the problem is beyond my authority, I'll escalate it to a supervisor or the appropriate department.

Interviewer: Can you tell us about a time when you went above and beyond for a customer?

Candidate: One time, I received a call from a customer who was frustrated because their package had been delayed multiple times. I contacted the shipping company and discovered the box had been lost in transit. I went above and beyond by contacting the manufacturer, who could send a replacement package directly to the customer. The customer was extremely grateful, and I was happy that I could resolve the problem to their satisfaction.

Interviewer: Thank you for your answers. Is there anything you would like to ask us?

Candidate: Yes, I would like to know more about this company's training and development opportunities for customer service representatives.

Interviewer: We provide a comprehensive training program for new employees and ongoing training to help representatives stay updated with the latest industry practices. We also offer opportunities for advancement within the company for employees who excel in their roles.

40. contoh job interview jurusan APHP​


Jawaban:

Pertanyaan Interview Bahasa Inggris

Okay, Please introduce yourself briefly? (Baiklah, Tolong perkenalkan diri anda secara singkat?)

Ini adalah pertanyaan paling mendasar yang biasa disodorkan dalam wawancara dengan menggunakan Bahasa Inggris. Artinya sederhana, yaitu meminta kamu untuk memperkenalkan dirimu secara singkat saja. Namun, ada sesuatu yang digali dari seorang yang mewawancara dari pertanyaan ini, yaitu tentang bagaimana kamu bisa membawa diri dan mengenalkan diri kamu secara baik dan benar. Berikut ini contoh dialognya:

You: My name is Rahman, I am twenty five years old. I have graduated from Gadjah Mada University, majoring in Computer Science. My Activity right now is writing on my Personal English Blog, I am also active in several communities that related to my background.

Anda: Nama saya Rahman, umur saya 25 tahun. Saya lulus kuliah dari Universitas Gadjah Mada, jurusan Komputer Sains. Aktivitas saya saat ini adalah menulis di blog berbahasa inggris pribadi saya, saya juga aktif di beberapa komunitas yang berhubungan dengan latar belakang saya.

Perkenalkan diri kamu dan bisa sedikit menyangkutkan dengan hal yang berkaitan dengan posisi yang ingin kamu lamar. Bisa juga ditambahkan dengan keterampilan yang diperlukan oleh pekerjaan impianmu tersebut.

What department do you apply and why do you interested on it? (Posisi apa yang anda lamar dan kenapa anda tertarik melamar posisi itu?)

Selanjutnya, setelah mengenalkan diri kamu secara singkat, biasanya pertanyaan selanjutnya dalam wawancara Bahasa Inggris adalah mengenai bagian apa yang kamu lamar dan mengapa kamu tertarik dengan posisi tersebut. Pertanyaan ini guna mencari tahu, apa motivasi kamu tentang pekerjaan yang ingin diambil. Apakah kamu serius untuk mengambilnya atau hanya sekedar main-main saja. Adapun contoh dialognya seperti berikut ini:

You: I want to apply for the System Analyst position, I am interested working on that position because that is my passion and I am also love working with computers since I was a kid.

Anda: Saya ingin melamar di posisi System Analyst, Saya tertarik bekerja pada posisi tersebut karena saya itulah semangat saya dan saya juga suka bekerja dengan komputer sejak saya masih kecil.

Kamu harus menjelaskan bagian apa yang kamu lamar dan alasan yang membuat kamu tertarik pada posisi tersebut. Berikan alasan pendukung yang akan membuat pewawancara yakin bahwa kamu pantas ada di posisi tersebut.
