if+ subject + VI (Simple Present Tense) + Subject + will + Verb-1

Menghibur pendengar. Mengajarkan pesan moral. Untuk menginspirasi pendengar.Untuk mengekspresikan perasaan pribadi. Untuk menyampaikan kritik sosial.

Intro: awal lagu, biasanya berisi musik saja atau vokal saja.Verse: pengantar ke chorus, nada sama namun beda lirik.Chorus: bagian yang paling sering diulang - ulang. Bridge: perubahn tempo (instrumentation)Coda: akhir dari lagu.

The song "Rise Up" by Andra Day follows a typical verse-chorus structure, with an instrumental bridge and a final chorus. The structure of the song can be outlined as follows:

- Intro

- Verse 1

- Chorus 1

- Verse 2

- Chorus 2

- Bridge

- Chorus 3

- Outro

The lyrics and melody of the verses and choruses are different, but they follow a similar pattern. The verses feature a lower and more subdued melody, while the choruses have a more uplifting and powerful melody, reflecting the theme of the song's message of empowerment and hope. The bridge serves as a musical interlude that builds up the energy and emotion of the song, leading to the final chorus, which repeats the main message of the song and ends on a strong and optimistic note.
