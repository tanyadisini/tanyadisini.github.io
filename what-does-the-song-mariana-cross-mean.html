2. The lyric conveys a message of support and reassurance, suggesting that the singer is there for someone whenever they need a friend. It expresses a sense of reliability, comfort, and companionship.

3. The song tells about the importance of having someone by your side during difficult times, offering unconditional support and friendship. It emphasizes the power of friendship and the willingness to be there for others.

4. From the song, one can learn the significance of empathy, compassion, and being a reliable friend. It highlights the value of offering support to those in need and being a source of comfort and strength.

5. "Diminishing" means decreasing or becoming less over time. In the context of the song, "and your spirit diminishing" suggests that someone's inner strength or motivation is gradually fading or getting weaker. It implies that the person may be feeling discouraged or losing hope.

Jawaban:

1. different artinya adalah berbeda
Contoh kalimat:
Your house is different to mines.

2. natural artinya adalah alami
Contoh kalimat:
Your look is really natural.

3. cume artinya adalah manis
Contoh kalimat:
That sugar is really cume.

4. live artinya adalah tinggal (tinggal di rumah)
Contoh kalimat:

I live in Bekasi.

5. warm artinya adalah hangat
Contoh kalimat:
That tea is warm.

6. tiny artinya adalah sangat kecil  
Contoh kalimat:
The ant is tiny.

7. hole artinya adalah lubang
Contoh kalimat:
I always see a small hole in my bag.

8. inside artinya adalah di dalam (suatu gedung / rumah / bangunan)
Contoh kalimat:
My sister is always inside her room doing homework.

9. squeeze artinya adalah memencet (suatu benda) / meremas

Contoh kalimat:
I'm going to squeeze an orange to make juice.

10. mop up artinya adalah membersihkan cairan tumpah
Contoh kalimat:
My mom is mopping up all the spilled water.
