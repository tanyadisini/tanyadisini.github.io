14. My brother ..... In the bank For more then Five years


My brother has been working in the Bank for more then five years.

Pendahuluan

Present Perfect Continuous Tense merupakan suatu tense untuk menyatakan suatu tindakan yang dimulai pada masa lampau dan masih berpengaruh sampai sekarang.

Rumus Present Perfect Tense terbagi menjadi 2, yaitu:

*Rumus Verbal(+) S + Have/Has + been + V1 -ing + O(-) S + Have/Has + not + been + V1 -ing + O(?) Have/Has + S + been + V1 -ing + O

*Rumus Nominal(+) S + Have/Has + been + ANA(-) S + Have/Has + not + been + ANA(?) Have/Has + S + been + ANA

Kata keterangan waktu yang digunakan:

For.Since.

Penyelesaian

Soal:

My brother ... in the Bank for more then five years

Jawaban:

Has been working.

Jawaban diatas menggunakan rumus verbal, sehingga menjadi:

My brother has been working in the Bank for more then five years

KesimpulanHave digunakan untuk subjek I, You, We dan TheyHas digunakan untuk subjek She, He dan ItSingkatan dari ANA adalah Adjectives/Noun/Adverb

28. what is the function of riddle​


Riddles are questions or statements that offer a puzzle to be solved. They often involve critical thinking on the reader’s part in order to figure out the answer, and this offers challenging entertainment.

Explanation :

What is the function of riddles?

While riddles often serve the function of entertaining the audience by proposing challenging questions to be solved, they also serve other purposes. For instance, they may allow for deeper thinking regarding an issue or to allow other questions to arise. By using riddles in these situations, it requires the reader to think of several possibilities in a critical manner rather than a superficial reading.
