[tex] \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin 2x \tan 6x}{3x^2} = \frac{1}{3} \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\frac{mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x} [\sin 2x \sin 6x]}{\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}[\cos 6x \cdot x^2]} [/tex]

[tex] \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin 2x \tan 6x}{3x^2} = \frac{1}{3} \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{2 \cos 2x \sin 6x + 6 \cos 6x \sin 2x}{2x \cos 6x - 6x^2 \sin x} [/tex]

[tex] \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin 2x \tan 6x}{3x^2} = \frac{1}{3} \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\cos 2x \sin 6x + 3 \cos 6x \sin 2x}{x \cos 6x - 3x^2 \sin x} [/tex]

[tex] \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin 2x \tan 6x}{3x^2} = \frac{1}{3} \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x} [\cos 2x \sin 6x + 3 \cos 6x \sin 2x]}{\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x} [x \cos 6x - 3x^2 \sin x]} [/tex]

[tex] \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin 2x \tan 6x}{3x^2} = \frac{1}{3} \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{6 \cos 2x \cos 6x - 2 \sin 2x \sin 6x + 6 \cos 2x \cos 6x - 6 \sin 6x \sin 2x}{\cos 6x - x \sin 6x - 6x \sin x - 3x^2 \cos 6x} [/tex]

[tex] \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin 2x \tan 6x}{3x^2} = \frac{12}{3} [/tex]

31. lim x->0 1-cosx/xsin2x Limit fungsi trigonometri


Jawab:

1/2

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Identitas trigonometri

[tex]cos A- cosB=-2sin\frac{1}{2}(A+B)sin\frac{1}{2}(A-B)[/tex]

Beberapa sifat limit fungsi

[tex]\lim_{x \to a} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)}= \frac{\lim_{x \to a} f(x)}{\lim_{x \to a} g(x)}\\ \lim_{x \to a} f(x).g(x)= \lim_{x \to a} f(x). \lim_{x \to a}g(x)[/tex]

Salah satu sifat limit fungsi trigonometri

[tex]\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{sinx}{x}= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x}{sinx}=1[/tex]

Dalil L'Hopital

[tex]\lim_{x \to a} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} =\lim_{x \to a} \frac{ f'(x)}{g'(x)}[/tex]

Sehingga

Kita masukan langsung nilai limitnya

[tex]\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1-cosx}{xsinx}\\=\frac{1-cos0}{0sin0}\\= \frac{0}{0}[/tex]

Karena menghasilkan bentuk tak tentu [tex]\frac{0}{0}[/tex] maka

(1) Cara 1 (manipulatif aljabar)

[tex]\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{cos0-cosx}{xsinx}\\= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{-2sin\frac{1}{2}(x)sin\frac{1}{2}(-x)}{xsinx}\\= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{-2sin\frac{x}{2}sin\frac{-x}{2}}{xsinx}\\= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{-2}{1}.\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{sin\frac{x}{2}}{x}.\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{sin\frac{-x}{2}}{sinx}\\= -2.\frac{1}{2}.-\frac{1}{2}\\\boxed{=\frac{1}{2}}[/tex]

(2) Cara 2 (DalilL'Hopital)

[tex]\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1-cosx}{xsinx}\\=\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\frac{d}{dx}(1-cosx)}{\frac{d}{dx}(xsinx)}\\=\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{0+sinx}{sinx+xcosx}\\=\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\frac{d}{dx}(sinx)}{\frac{d}{dx}(sinx+xcosx)}\\=\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{cosx}{cosx+cosx-xsinx}\\=\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{cosx}{2cosx-xsinx}\\=\frac{cos0}{2cos0-0sin0}\\\boxed{=\frac{1}{2}}[/tex]

39. tentukan nilai limit trigonometri berikut limit x mendekati 0 tan x per x​


Jawaban:

[tex]lim \: \frac{ \tan(x) }{x} = lim \: \frac{ \frac{ \sin(x) }{ \cos(x) } }{x} \\ = lim \: \frac{ \sin(x) }{ \cos(x) \times x } \\ = lim \: \frac{1}{ \cos(x) } \times \frac{ \sin(x) }{x} \\ = \frac{1}{ \cos(0) } \times 1 \\ = \frac{1}{1} \times 1 \\ = 1[/tex]

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex] \lim_{x \to0}( \frac{ \tan(x) }{x} ) [/tex]

karena nilai tan (0) = 0 dan x = 0 maka kita terapkan teori L'hopital.

[tex] \lim_{x \to0}( \frac{ \frac{d}{dx} \tan(x) }{ \frac{d}{dx} x} ) [/tex]

[tex] \lim_{x \to0}( \frac{ \sec^{2} (x) }{ 1} ) [/tex]

[tex] \lim_{x \to0}( \sec^{2} (x) ) [/tex]

[tex] = \sec^{2} (0) [/tex]

[tex] = {1}^{2} [/tex]

[tex] = 1[/tex]
