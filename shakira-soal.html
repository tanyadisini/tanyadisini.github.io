25. Shane, Shannon, and Shakira share $90 among them. Shannon receives $7 more than Shane, while Shakira receives S4 less than Shane. Find the amount of money they receive respectively. Answer.please answer using linear equations​


misal shane : x, shannon :y, Shakira : z

x + y + z = 90

x + 7 = y

=> x = y - 7

x - 4 = z

=> z = x - 4

x + y + z = 90

(y - 7) + y + ((y - 7) - 4) = 90

3y - 18 = 90

3y = 108

y = 36

x = 36 - 7

x = 29

z = 29 - 4

z = 25

jadi uang yang diterima

shane : 29$

Shannon : 36$

Shakira : 25$
