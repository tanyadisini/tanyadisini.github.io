16. Change the verb in the bracket into correct tense. A) the students were cleaning the room while the teacher (meet) at the office. B) they were studying when the teacher (come) into the room


Subject : English

Category : Tenses (Past Simple and Past Continuous)

Level : JHS VIII

Keywords : when, while

Code : 5


Past Simple Tense is used to describe an event that happened and completed in the past.

Pattern : S + Preterite (v₂)


The Past Continuous is used to describe actions that were happening at a specified time the past. It is also used to describe something that was happening when another action interrupted it in the past.

Structure : S + was/were + present participle


Past continuous and past simple are often used together. The past continuous describes the longer or background action while the past simple describes the action that interrupts the background action.

ANSWERS :

A) The students were cleaning the room while the teacher was meeting at the office.

B) they were studying when the teacher came into the room.
