35 Contoh Soal Modals Can, Could, Will dan Would

Written By DBI Wednesday, January 23, 2019 12 Comments

Modals adalah bagian dari auxiliary verbs.

Modals memiliki beberapa jenis, diantaranya adalah can, could, will dan would.

Masing-masing dari modals tersebut memiliki fungsi dan aturannya masing-masing.

Untuk melatih pemahaman mengenai materi modals can, could, will dan would, berikut beberapa soal latihan tentang modals can, could, will dan would.

Penjelasan lengkap mengenai aturan dan fungsi modals Can, Could, Will dan Would dapat diakses pada link berikut:

Aturan Dan Fungsi Modals Will, Would, Can, Could

EXERCISE 1

Circle the Correct Modal in Each Sentence (Can, Can't, Could and Couldn't).

1. We (can - can't - could - couldn't) speak English well because our grandfather is a British.

2. A year ago, Laura (can - can't - could - couldn't) get in contact with her brother because he changed his number.

3. (can - can't - could - couldn't) you join me to go to Lisa's birthday party tomorrow? It'll be fun if we go together.

4. I was smart before. I (can - can't - could - couldn't) do algebra when I was 8 years old.

5. (can - can't - could - couldn't) you play piano for me?

6. Mr. Young (can - can't - could - couldn't) attend the meeting because he's sick.

7. Naura is wearing glasses. She (can - can't - could - couldn't) see things clearly.

8. Rianti was very busy. She (can - can't - could - couldn't) exercise regularly.

9. The building is close. We (can - can't - could - couldn't) come inside.

10. The rain (can - can't - could - couldn't) be worse this afternoon.

DBI | Latihan Can Could Will Would

EXERCISE 2

Underline the Correct Modal in the Sentence Below (Will, Won't, Would and Would not).

1. I (will - won't - would - wouldn't) buy new bag if I had enough money.

2. My mom (will - won't - would - wouldn't) fly to Singapore. She has an invitation to attend.

3. Ruli and Yuna (will - won't - would - wouldn't) celebrate their birthday together because it's on the same day.

4. Raisa (will - won't - would - wouldn't) lend Fay money. Fay never pay back.

5. (will - won't - would - wouldn't) you like pizza or pasta for dinner?

6. My baby (will - won't - would - wouldn't) sleep well last night. I'm tired and sleepy now.

7. Dad (will - won't - would - wouldn't) come home early. He's stuck in traffic jam.

8. I (will - won't - would - wouldn't) like to eat dessert, please!

9. (will - won't - would - wouldn't) you mind helping me with my stuff? It's so heavy.

10. I'm tired. I think I (will - won't - would - wouldn't) go to bed now.

Jawaban:

4. could

5. can

6. can't

7. can

8. couldn't

Penjelasan:

Perlu kita ketahui perbedaan dari can dan could, bahwa:

Arti dari can dan could, sama—hanya penggunaannya berbeda.

Penggunaan can/could pada teks di atas:Dia pandai aljabar hanya saat dia berusia 8 tahun. Mungkin, setelah itu, dia tidak pandai aljabar sepandai saat dia kecil. Jadi, kita gunakan verba 3 dari can yaitu could.Dia meminta temannya untuk memainkan piano, yang kita ketahui; temannya bisa bermain piano, kalau tidak, dia tidak akan bertanya seperti itu. Jadi, kita gunakan, canMr. Young sedang sakit dan hal itu berakibatnya tidak dapat mengikuti rapat, jadi kita gunakan can't, kalau Mr. Young tidak sakit, pastinya dia dapat mengikuti rapat, can.Naura bisa melihat dengan jelas karena kacamatanya, jadi kita gunakan canKarena kata kuncinya was dan busy, maka kita gunakan couldn't. Karena kesibukannya membuatnya tidak dapat berolahraga secara biasanya.
