EXPLANATION

Passive voice is used when we want to emphasize the action and recipient of the verb.

The steps how to change the active voice to the passive voice are :

~ pay attention to the verb form whether it's transitive or not.

~ the object of active voice is moved to the subject of passive voice. Meanwhile, the subject of active voice is changed into the object of passive voice accompanied with the "by" preposition or sometimes it's omitted.

~ all of the verbs are changed into the past participle verb (V³).

New subject(The prime minister)+verb(was asked+D.O (a lot of difficult questions)+by+Agent(Journalists).

Subject (Nobody)+verb(has told)+Object(me)+Noun clause(what to do).

changes to :

New subject(I)+verb(haven't been told)+Noun clause(what to do).

Hence, it can be omitted and the noun clause verb is changed into the negative form (haven't been told).Since the word "nobody" is categorized in the indefinite pronoun and it doesn't refer to the specific person, it can be eliminated.
