9. procedure text how to make omelet dan artinya


Procedure Text

JAWABAN:

How to make an omelette

Ingredients

2 eggs Onion, roughly chopped - 2 tablespoons Paprika, cut into squares - 2 tablespoons Tomato. cut into boxes - 2 tablespoons Champignon mushroom, thinly sliced ​​- 2 pieces Sausage, thinly sliced ​​- 2 pieces Mozzarella or cheddar cheese melts quickly, cut into squares - 1 tablespoon Cheddar cheese, grated - 1 tablespoonLiquid milk - 3 tablespoons Salt - 1/2 tsp Pepper - 1/4 tsp Margarine, for sauteing - 2 tablespoons

The steps

In a container, beat the eggs with grated cheese, liquid milk, salt, and pepper until they are even. Set aside. Heat margarine. Saute onions, peppers, mushrooms, and sausages. Cook until fragrant bombai and cooked mushrooms. Flatten the ingredients throughout the pan. Reduce the fire. Pour the beaten eggs and cook until half cooked. Add the tomatoes and checkered cheese. Fold the omelette into a half circle shape. Cover the pan briefly and cook until the cheese has melted. Lift. Ready to be served.

TIPS

Omelette can be created with filling according to taste, such as smoked meat, shredded chicken, and spinach. Use a non-stick pan, so you can make perfectly round omelets that are evenly cooked. Make sure the bottom or outside of the omelette is rather dry so that it doesn't break when folded.

PEMBAHASAN:

Procedure Text

Tujuan dari teks prosedur adalah untuk memberitahu pembaca bagaimana melakukan atau membuat sesuatu melalui urutan tindakan atau langkah-langkah.

“The purpose of procedure text is to tell the reader how to do or make something through a sequence of actions or steps.”

Generic Structure of Procedure Text

Procedure text memiliki generic structure. Berikut ini generic structure of procedure text:

Goal: memberikan informasi tentang maksud dan tujuan prosedur dan memprediksi suatu kesimpulan.  (provides information about the purpose and objectives of the procedure and predicts a conclusion)Materials: berisi daftar materi atau bahan-bahan yang dibutuhkan untuk melakukan suatu prosedur atau langkah-langkah.  (contains a list of materials or materials needed to carry out a procedure or steps)Steps: daftar urutan instruksi/aktivitas untuk mencapai tujuan dalam urutan langkah yang benar.  (list the sequence of instructions / activities to reach the destination in the correct sequence of steps)

Language features of Procedure Text

Mengunakan Simple Present Tense (S+V1) Berbentuk imperative/bentuk perintah, Menggunakan action verbs, contoh: make, take, boil, cook, Menggunakan temporal conjunctions, contoh: First, then, next, after that, last

11. Procudure text how to make omelet


Jawaban:

Title: How to Make a Basic Omelet

Ingredients:

- 2-3 large eggs

- Salt and pepper to taste

- 1-2 tablespoons of butter or cooking oil

- Fillings (e.g., cheese, diced vegetables, ham, or herbs) (optional)

Equipment:

- Frying pan

- Whisk or fork

- Spatula

- Bowl

Procedure:

1. **Prepare Your Fillings (Optional):** If you're adding ingredients like cheese, vegetables, or ham, chop or grate them into small pieces and set them aside.

2. **Crack and Beat the Eggs:** Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add a pinch of salt and a dash of pepper for flavor. Use a whisk or fork to beat the eggs until the yolks and whites are well combined.

3. **Heat the Pan:** Place the frying pan on the stovetop over medium-low heat. Add the butter or cooking oil and let it melt, ensuring it coats the entire pan evenly.

4. **Pour the Eggs into the Pan:** Once the butter or oil is hot and sizzling, pour the beaten eggs into the pan. Allow them to cook without stirring for a minute or two until the edges start to set.

5. **Add Fillings (Optional):** If you're using fillings, now is the time to sprinkle them over one-half of the omelet.

6. **Fold the Omelet:** Carefully fold the other half of the omelet over the side with the fillings using a spatula. Press down gently to ensure it sticks together.

7. **Cook Until Set:** Continue cooking the omelet for another minute or two until it's no longer runny in the center. You can adjust the cooking time to achieve your desired level of doneness.

8. **Slide Onto a Plate:** Once the omelet is cooked to your liking, gently slide it onto a plate using the spatula.

9. **Serve:** Your basic omelet is ready to be served. You can garnish it with fresh herbs, additional cheese, or a side of toast, if desired.

10. **Enjoy:** Enjoy your homemade omelet while it's hot!

Remember, this is just a basic omelet recipe. You can get creative with your fillings and toppings to suit your taste preferences.

17. cara buat how to make cheese cake


how to make the perfect cheesecake.

1) Use a springform pan and a water bath. The springform pan works best because it lets you remove the cake easily and the water bath prevents unsightly cracking on the top of your cheesecake.

2) When mixing ingredients, mix evenly to ensure that all lumps in the cream cheese are eliminated before adding the eggs. Eggs generally bring air in the batter, so add them last and stir as little as possible once the eggs have been added.

3) Do not overbeat the cake batter. Once the ingredients are mixed well, stop stirring.

4) Bake the cheesecake at the recommended oven temperature using a water bath. To avoid water seepage, wrap the springform cake pan in foil about halfway up the side of the springform pan. Set the springform pan wrapped in foil in a larger pan containing hot water. The level of the water should not be higher than the foil around the springform pan.

5) Watch your baking time carefully and avoid over-baking your cheesecake. There are several successful methods for determining when to remove the cheesecake from the oven: 1.) When it is slightly moist in the center, 2.) When it has reached an ideal temperature of 160-165 degrees or, 3.) When a small knife inserted in the center comes out clean. If the cheesecake is about done and you are worried about over cooking it, you may turn off the oven and let it sit in the oven for a little while longer to ensure that the cake sets completely.

6) After removing the cheesecake from the oven, run a knife around the edge of the cheesecake. This helps to loosen the cake from the edge of the pan, allowing it to pull away freely as it cools.

7) Allow the cheesecake to cool completely. The cooling step is as important as proper mixing and baking. You should cool the cheesecake at room temperature, away from drafts. Once the cheesecake has cooled completely you should place the cheesecake (pan and all) in the refrigerator for at least 6 hours or overnight. Once it’s thoroughly cooled, it will be much easier to remove the springform pan and it is ready to serve to your guests.

3.Use an 8-inch nonstick skillet for a 2-egg omelet, a 9-inch skillet for 3 eggs.

4.Melt the butter over medium-low heat, and keep the temperature low and slow when cooking the eggs so the bottom doesn’t get too brown or overcooked.

5.Use a heatproof silicone spatula to gently lift the cooked eggs from the edges of the pan.

6.Tilt the pan to allow the uncooked eggs to flow to the edge of the pan.

7.Add the filling—but don’t overstuff the omelet—when the eggs begin to set.

8.Cook for a few more seconds

9.Fold the omelet in half.

10.Slide it onto a plate with the help of a silicone spatula.

32. arrange the correct steps to make a cheese omelet! please gak tau cara buat nya


1. Crack and put the eggs in a bowl and beat well until uniform yellow color.(usually, for every 3 eggs, put 2 yolks, more yolks means more cholesterol).
2. Add a pinch of salt.
3. Heat a pan and add vegetable oil or butter (butter being more fattening).
4. Get the omelette ingredients ready and hand-toss in a separate bowl.
5. Pour egg into pan and let cook for about 7 seconds (the hotter the pan, the shorter you wait), do not fold the egg or touch the egg with a spatula during the 7 seconds.
6. Pour omelette contents into center of still liquid egg part.
7. Fold the "edges" of the egg over onto itself to cook the cheese and other contents. After about 20 seconds of total cooking time, use the spatula and partially dice the contents and stir-fry like normal.
8. Take pan off once the egg has turned solid, but before it becomes browned.
9. Serve hot
