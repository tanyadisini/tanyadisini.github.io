20. bob and mike .....(not swim)because it's too cold


Cause and Effect

Cause and Effect yang sering juga disebut sebagai kausalitas, adalah kalimat yang menunjukan akibat yang ditimbulkan atau hasil dari suatu tindakan lain.

Kata-kata yang menunjulkan sebuah Cause and Effect adalah:

1. Because

2. Since

3. For

4. Due to

5. Because of

6. Therefore

7. as

8. So, dan lain lain

NB:

Untuk penulisan because, since, dan for harus diikuti sebuah kalimat. Contoh:

The flight is delayed because/since/for the weather is bad.

Sedangkan untuk due to dan because of, harus diikuti frasa kata benda (noun phrase).

Contoh:

The flight is delayed due to/ because of a bad weather.

Contoh lain kalimat cause and effect:

1. I help you because I like you.

2. I study hard because I want to pass the exam.

3. We have to go home since we have no idea where to go next.

4. You need to hurry up since it’s very late now.

5. I can’t go out as it’s raining.

6. I can’t run fast as I’m too fat.

7. I love you, so I will do everything to make you happy.

8. I didn’t eat anything, so I’m very hungry now.

9. We had to cancel the picnic because of bad weather.

10. I did it because of you.

11. The flight was canceled due to the storm.

12. I came home late due to the traffic jam.

13. He didn’t work hard. As a result, he was fired.

14. I was busy this evening. Therefore, I couldn’t come to your party.

25. PracticeA. Write these sentences using subject and objectPronouns instead of the underlined words.1.david and mike are arriving today. i'm meeting david and mike at the station​


Jawaban:

pronoun (kata ganti) digunakan untuk mengganti subyek atau obyek agar tidak terjadi pengulangan kata.

misalnya:

Rani gets up at 5 am. Then, she takes a bath.

subyek di kalimat pertama adalah Rani. Subyek di kalimat kedua juga Rani, tapi ditulis dengan pronoun she agar tidak ada pengulangan kata Rani sehingga lebih enak dibaca.

subject pronoun artinya kata ganti yang berfungsi sebagai subyek kalimat. contoh:

She writes a love letter.

kata she ini adalah subject pronoun.

object pronoun artinya kata ganti yang berfungai sebagai obyek kalimat. contoh:

I give him money.

kata him ini adalah object pronoun.

di lampiran gambar bisa dilihat kata-kata ganti dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Jawaban soal:

1. David and Mike are arriving today. I'm meeting David and Mike at the station.

subyek kalimat pertama adalah David and Mike. kata ganti subyek (subject pronoun) yang tepat adalah they, karena subyek lebih dari satu orang.

Kalimat kedua, David and Mike berfungsi sebagai obyek, maka kata ganti obyek (object pronoun) yang tepat adalah them.

1. They are arriving today. I'm meeting them at the station.

Present continuous tense dibentuk dengan rumus:
