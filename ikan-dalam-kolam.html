12 ÷ 2 = p + 2

6 = p + 2

p = 6 - 2

= 4 m

L = p × l

= 4 × 2

= 8 m²

Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Balok

Panjang (P) = 6 m

Lebar (L) = (1/2)P = (1/2)6 = 3 m

Tinggi (T) = (1/3)P = (1/3)6 = 2 m

Volume (V) = P×L×T = 6×3×2 =

36 m³

Volumenya 36 meter kubik

atau

36 × 1.000.000 cm³ =

36.000.000 cm³

Volumenya 36 juta centimeter kubik
