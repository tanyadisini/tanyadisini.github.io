15. jika 2 losin sendok brarti 24 buah sendok,maka 2 mol air brarti? ​


Dik : n = 2 mol

Ar H = 1

Ar O = 16

Dit : gram.....???

Mol (n) = gram /(Mr/Ar)

gram = n x Mr/Ar (H2O)

= n x (2H + 1O)

= 2 x (2(1) + 1(16))

= 2 x (2 + 16)

= 2 x 18

gram = 36 gram

Jadi 2 mol air berarti 36 gram air
