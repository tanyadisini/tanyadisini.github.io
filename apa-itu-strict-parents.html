1. strict parents artinya adalah...


Jawaban:

Strict parents tersusun dari dua kata, yaitu strict dan parents. Kata strict termasuk ke dalam kata sifat atau adjective, sedangkan parents termasuk ke dalam kata benda atau noun.

Strict artinya secara keras membatasi kebebasan seseorang untuk bersikap atau cenderung menghukum dengan keras apabila seseorang tidak patuh.

Kata tersebut juga dapat didefinisikan sebagai seseorang yang mengikuti peraturan atau suatu paham/prinsip dengan sangat ketat/taat.

Secara sederhana, Strict parents adalah Orang Tua yang Kaku.

Definition/Classification ...................................................................... 2. Detail Desription .................................................................


Explanation:

Descriptive text is a kind of text which is used to describe a specific person, thing, or place.

Function: The function of the descriptive text is to explain about a specific person, thing, or place to the listeners or the readers so they can understand and imagine it although they never see it before.

The structure of descriptive text:
- Definition/classification, this part contains a general description of what we want to tell to the listeners or the readers.
-.Detail description, this part contains further explanation regarding the topic that has been introduced in the previous part.

Answer:
Here's the generic structure of your text:

My Parents
Definition/classification: My father is a tailor. My mother is a housewife. sometimes she helps out in the shop. my parents are very strict.

Detail description: they make sure l finish my homework before l watch television. they also teach me to be honest. when l do well in my examination, my parents reward me. they let me buy any toy that like. even though my parents are strict, they are kind.l love my parents very much.
