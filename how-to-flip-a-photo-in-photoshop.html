5. jelaskan apa yang dimaksud flip-flop berikut ini!a. flip-flop RSb. flip-flop JKc. flip-flop D​


Jawab:

a.) Flipflop RS  adalah dasar dari semua Flip-flop yang memiliki 2 gerbang inputan / masukan yaitu R dan S. R artinya “RESET” dan S artinya “SET”. Flip-flop yang satu ini mempunyai 2 keluaran / outputyaitu Q dan Q`

b.)  flip-flop JK adalah flip flop yang dibangun berdasarkan pengembangan dari RS flip-flop. JK flip-flop sering diaplikasikan sebagai komponen dasar suatu counter atau pencacah naik (up counter) ataupun pencacah turun (down counter)

c.)Flip-flop D adalah modifikasi dari S-R Flip-flip yaitu dengan menambahkan gerbang logika NOT (Inverter) dari Input S ke Input R. Berbeda dengan S-R Flip-flop, D Flip-flop hanya mempunyai satu Input yaitu Input atau Masukan D.

7. - Apa logika tambahan diperlukan untuk mengubah flip-flop JK menjadi D flip-flop? - Apa itu register geser dan mengapa ini sangat penting dalam digital sistem? - Explain why it is necessary to employ clocked flip-flops in sequential circuits (as opposed to unclocked flip-flops)?


Apa logika tambahan diperlukan untuk  mengubah flip-flop JK menjadi D flip-flop?  Apa itu register geser dan mengapa ini sangat penting dalam digital  sistem?Explain why it is necessary to employ clocked flip-flops in  sequential circuits (as opposed to unclocked flip-flops)? JawabanPendahuluan

Pada rangkaian elektronika digital, Flip-Flop atau latch merupakan sirkuit elektronik yang memiliki rangkaian penyimpan tegangan masukan yang dapat dimanfaatkan untuk menyimpan informasi. Flip-flop dan latch merupakan komponen penting dalam sistem elektronika digital yang digunakan pada komputer dan sistem digital lainnya.

Flip-flop elektronik pertama ditemukan pada tahun 1918 oleh William Eccless dan F. W. Jordan. Awalnya dinamai Sirkuit Trigger Eccles-Jordan dan berisi dua elemen aktif (tabung vakum). Seperti versi sirkuit dan transistornya yang sering dijumpai pada komputer, setelah penemuan dari IC flip-flop dibuat dari gerbang logika yang kita kenal sekarang.


Pembahasan

1. Untuk mengubah JK flip flop menjadi D flip flop, kita perlu menambahkan gerbang NOT yang inputnya dipasang pada pin J dan output gerbang not di pasang pada pin K di JK flip flop tersebut.

2. Register geser atau shift register adalah suatu komponen elektronik yang memiliki banyak output dengan sebuah input. Register geser ini bekerja dengan 2 buah pin input yaitu data dan clock. Ketika clock diberi teganan dari high ke low ke high lagi, maka artinya 1 clock telah diberikan ke register geser, dan tegangan pada pin output pertama akan menjadi seperti data. Kemudian jika 1 clock diberikan lagi, pin output 2 akan memiliki tegangan seperti pin output 1 dan pin output satu akan memiliki tegangan seperti pin input data. Hal ini berlaku untuk semua pin output, setiap diberikan 1 clock akan memiliki kondisi seperti pin output sebelumnya. Atau bisa kita tuliskan.

O(n) = O(n-1), if n = 0, O(n) = input data.

Register geser ini akan sangat penting dalam sistem digital karena sering sekali digunakan dalam metode scanning matrix, dimana dalam metode ini menggunakan register geser untuk membaca dan atau mengganti kondisi tegangan pada sebuah titik pada matrix. Selain itu register geser juga digunakan sebagai konverter data serial ke paralel. Metode konversi yang dilakukan dari serial menjadi paralel yaitu dengan memberikan data biner secara serial (high low), kemudian diikuti clock setiap bit datanya. Data akan menjadi memiliki antarmuka paralel pada pin output. Misalnya data serial adalah angka 10, yaitu binernya 00001010, akan diubah menjadi paralel sehingga pin output 0 sampai pin output 8 akan bernilai 00001000. Dimana 0 merepresentasikan kondisi tegangan LOW dan 1 merepresentasikan tegangan HIGH. Maka cara pengkoversiannya adalah dengan memberikan setiap bit data mulai dari LSB (Least significant bit) ke MSB (Most significant bit) kemudian di beri 1 clock sehingga pin output akan menjadi data paralel dari input setelah diclock 8 kali. Contoh ini jika data yang diberikan berukuran 8 bit.

3. With clocked flip-flop we can easily manage sequential output condition just giving a clock to the clock input and changing the input as we need. If we using unclocked flip-flops, it could be so hard to change the desired condition because we need to change the input to give a "clock" while set the input to give the next condition as well.

15. 3 steps to write photo caption Wild art (definition and example) Punctuation and capitalization in photo caption Photo caption and cutline


Jawaban:

**3 Steps to Write a Photo Caption:**

1. **Describe the Photo:** Begin by providing a concise and clear description of what's happening in the photo. Mention the main subject or action captured in the image.

2. **Add Context:** Give context to the photo by explaining when and where it was taken, who is in the photo, and why it's significant or interesting.

3. **Engage the Audience:** Make the caption engaging by adding a touch of creativity, humor, or emotion. You can also include relevant hashtags or mentions to increase the photo's reach on social media.

**Wild Art (Definition and Example):**

Wild art, often referred to as "wild art stories" in journalism, involves publishing eye-catching, unusual, or offbeat photographs or visuals that may not have a direct connection to a headline news story but are attention-grabbing on their own. These images are often used to break up the text-heavy nature of news publications and captivate the reader's interest.

Example of a Wild Art Caption: "Local artist creates stunning mural on abandoned building, adding a burst of color to the neighborhood."

**Punctuation and Capitalization in Photo Caption:**

- **Punctuation:** Use appropriate punctuation marks like commas, periods, and apostrophes where needed. For example, "Friends enjoying a day at the beach, basking in the sun."

- **Capitalization:** Capitalize the first letter of each sentence and proper nouns (names of people, places, etc.). For example, "Amanda and David explore the historic streets of Paris."

**Photo Caption and Cutline:**

A **photo caption** is a brief description or explanation of a photo, typically found directly beneath or beside the image. It provides context and information about what the viewer is seeing.

A **cutline** is essentially the same as a photo caption and can be used interchangeably. In some cases, "cutline" may be a term more commonly associated with print journalism, while "photo caption" is used in digital and online contexts. Both serve the purpose of helping the audience understand the photo better.

20. HOW TO OPERATE PHOTO COPY MACHINE BESERTA ARTINYA


A photocopy machine is one of the most important pieces of equipment in any business. As a business employee, you may be responsible for making hundreds of photocopies on a daily basis. You should not be embarrassed if you do not have experience with a photocopier, however. Many people view photocopy machines as large foreign objects. The following is a quick tutorial that can having you making copies in less than two minutes:

Turn on the Photocopier

The first step you will take in using the photocopier is making sure it is powered on a plugged up. First, check for plugs, and make sure that the copier’s cord is plugged into a nearby outlet. Next, look for the power button. Most copiers have the power button on top, but you may find the switch on the side. If you find that the unit is already on but is not responding, then it may be in “sleep” mode. In that case, you can press the “copy” button to see if it responds to that.

Before moving to the next step, you will want to make sure that the copier has paper in it. The paper tray will be located either on the front or the side of the device. Fill the tray with paper if you notice that it is empty or the level is low.

Put Your Document on the Copier

The next step is placing your document on the copier. You will lift the photocopier’s cover and place your document on the glass surface. You will want the front of the document facing downward on the glass. You will also want to place the document the way the guide marks tell you. You will see the guide marks once you lift the cover. After you have properly placed your document, you can close the copier cover.

Select the Number of Copies

You will need to select the number of copies that you want the copier to make. The unit will have a digital number display and a set of plus and minus keys so that you can change the quantity. Some models have numeric keys for this same function.

Select the Color Preference

Next, you will want to tell the copier whether you would like color copies or black and white copies. The unit will have a dedicated button for this selection. Press the button to make your changes.

Select Paper Size

After you select the color preference, you will want to select the correct paper size. 8.5 x 11 inches is the standard paper size. If you need to use a special paper size, then you will have to feed the paper into the tray.

Press the Copy Button

Finally, you will press the “copy” button to print your copies. Some units have the copy button labeled as a start button. Once you press the copy button, your copies will start printing. You may stand in front of the copier while is it printing so that you catch any errors immediately.
