A. No walk

Penjelasan:

karena di kalimat ada tulisan "they were very tired"

maka tense yang digunakan adalah PAST SIMPLE TENSE.

Past Simple Tense digunakan untuk menunjukkan kegiatan yang terjadi di masa lalu.

Formula untuk Past Simple Tense :

(+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object

example : He ate noodle yesterday.

(-) Subject + didn't + Verb-1 + Object

example : He didn't eat noodle yesterday.

(?) Did + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?

example : Did he eat noodle yesterday?

signal words untuk Past Simple Tense :

yesterday, last... , .... ago, this morning.
