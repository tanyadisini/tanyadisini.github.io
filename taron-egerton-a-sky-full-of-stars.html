17. Which figure of speech of these lines?a. I felt like a fish out of water.b The flowers danced in the gentle breezeC. The stars were diamonds in the sky​


Jawaban:

a.Simile

b.Personification

c.Metaphor

Penjelasan:

In line a,the usage of the word 'like' and the comparison made indicates that the line is a simile.

The figure of speech 'Personification' is when a human characteristic is given to something that is not human.In line b,the personification comes from the word 'danced'.flowers obviously cannot dance in the way humans do,but the word is used to describe the movements of the flowerss waving.

As for C,metaphor is quite similar to simile in many regards,but what mainly sets them apart is the use of the word 'like' or 'as' in simile.Since metaphors state direct comparisons and so did line C,therefore line C is a metaphor.

27. 3. "The sky is full of dancing stars” "The TV suddenly stopped talking" are the examples of........ A. Hyperbole B. Alliteration C. Metaphor D. Personification E. Simile​


Jawaban: D. Personification

Penjelasan:

The first example, "The sky is full of dancing stars," is an example of a metaphor, as it compares the sky to something else (a stage filled with dancers). The second example, "The TV suddenly stopped talking," is an example of personification, as it gives human-like qualities (talking) to a non-human object (the TV). Therefore, the answer is D. Personification.

37. Pecel:three starsSatay:four starsSemur jengkol:five starsof the data above, satay is........ pecel​


Jawaban:

Comparative Degree:

Based on the data above, satay is more delicious than pecel.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Comparative Degree (gelar perbandingan) karena memperbandingkan dua hal yang berbeda dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Berdasarkan data, sate mendapatkan penilaian bintang lebih banyak daripada pecel dari para penikmat kuliner Nusantara, sehingga bisa kita simpulkan bahwa sate lebih enak rasanya daripada pecel.

Comparative Degree:

Kita menambahkan akhiran "-er" dan "than" pada kata sifat satu suku kata dan awalan "more" dan "than" pada kata sifat lebih dari dua suku kata untuk membentuk Comparative Degree (gelar perbandingan), contohnya:

Bigger than = lebih besar daripada (satu suku kata dari "Big")

More Beautiful than = lebih cantik daripada (empat suku kata "Beautiful")

More Delicious than = lebih lezat daripada (lima suku kata "delicious)
