Cara menambahkan akhiran s atau es pada kata kerja:

Akhiran -es ditambahkan pada kata kerja (verb) yang berhuruf akhir -ch (catches), -s (kisses), -sh (brushes), -x (fixes), -z (whizzes) Akhiran -es juga ditambahkan pada kata kerja (veb) yang berhuruf akhir -y yang didahului oleh huruf mati dan akan berubah menjadi -i jika mendapat tambahan akhiran -es tersebut. contoh: study=studies, carry=carries, try=tries. 27. On Monday, Lisa and Denise bought some berries. Denise bought 4 times as many berries as Lisa. On Tuesday, Denise gave 132 berries away and Lisa bought 162 berries. They had the same number of berries in the end. How many berries did they buy altogether on Monday?​


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Let's call the number of berries Lisa bought on Monday "x".

Denise bought 4 times as many, so she bought 4x.

Together, they bought x + 4x = 5x berries.

On Tuesday, Denise gave away 132 berries, so she had 4x - 132 left.

Lisa bought 162 berries, so she had x + 162 berries.

We know that they ended up with the same number of berries, so:

4x - 132 = x + 162

Simplifying, we get:

3x = 294

x = 98

So Lisa bought 98 berries and Denise bought 4 times as many, or 392 berries.

Altogether, they bought 98 + 392 = 490 berries on Monday.
