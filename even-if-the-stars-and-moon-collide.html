3. The difference between simile and personification is:

Simile is a figure of speech that makes a comparison between two things using the words "like" or "as." It explicitly states that one thing is similar to another.
Example: "She runs as fast as a cheetah."Personification is a figure of speech that gives human characteristics or qualities to non-human entities or objects.
Example: "The flowers danced in the wind."

4. The figurative language in the song lyrics "bomm, bomm, even brighter than the moon moon moon" (from the song "Firework" by Katy Perry) is:

Repetition: The repeated use of the word "bomm" emphasizes the explosiveness and impact of the fireworks. The repetition of "moon moon moon" emphasizes the brightness and brilliance of the fireworks, comparing them to the moon.

mass of Earth
m1 = 6 × 10^(24) kg
mass of Moon
m2 = 7.4 × 10^(22) kg
distance between the earth and moon
r = 3.84 × 10^5 km = 3.84 × 10^8 m

the force exerted by the earth and the moon given by

F = G × m1 × m2 / r²
F = 6.67×10^(-11) × 6 × 10^(24) × 7.4 × 10^(22) / (3.84 × 10^8)²
