4. The song is called "Dear Mama" by Tupac Shakur. The message of the song is about the rapper's love and appreciation for his mother and how she has always been there for him through thick and thin. The song is a tribute to all mothers who have sacrificed so much for their children.

5. The verse is the part of the song where the lyrics are spoken rather than sung. In "Dear Mama," the verses are the parts where Tupac raps about his childhood and his mother's struggles.

The chorus is the part of the song that is repeated after each verse. In "Dear Mama," the chorus is the part where Tupac sings "Mama I love you, Mama I care, Mama I love you, Mama my friend."

6. The line "Every other day I crossed the line I didn't mean to be so bad" is an example of hyperbole, which is a figure of speech that uses exaggeration to make a point. In this case, Tupac is exaggerating how often he got into trouble as a child.

I Love You artinya Saya Cinta Kamu, entah untuk kemarin sekarang atau besok, tidak dibatasi oleh waktu. I Loved You mempunyai arti Saya Telah Mencintai Kamu, tetapi digunakan untuk bentuk lampau, perasaan yang telah berlalu dan tidak untuk sekarang. I Loving You mempunyai arti Saya Sedang Mencintai Kamu, maksudnya perasaan yang saat ini sedang dialami. 24. Kuis[+50]: ➕ ➖❔ ⬇️ ➕ I want you. ➖ ❔ ⬇️ ➕ ➖ She doesn't like you. ❔ ⬇️ ➕ ➖ ❔ Am I the only one you love? ⬇️ ➕ ➖ He is not interested in loving somebody. ❔


1. (+) = I Want you.

(-) = I Don't Want you.

(?) = Do i want you? (Bisa juga menjadi Do you want me?).

2. (+) = She likes you.

(-) = She doesn't Like you.

(?) = Does she like you?

3. (+) I am the only one you love.

(-) I am not the only one you love.

(?) Am i the only one you love?

4. (+) He is interested in loving somebody.

(-) He is not Interested in loving somebody.

(?) Is he interested in loving somebody?

Jawaban:

1.

(+) I want you.

(–) I don't want you (?) Do I want you?

2.

(+) she does like you

(–) She doesn't like you

(?) does she like you?

3. (+) I am the only one you Love (–) I am not the only one you love

(?) am I the only one you love?

4.

(+) He is interested in loving somebody.

(–) He is not interested in loving somebody

(?) is he interested in loving somebody?

Penjelasan :

(+) kalimat Positive (kalimat nya masih utuh)

(–) kalimat Negative (ada tambahan Not nya set. to be present.)

(?) Kalimat Intro Negative (ada tanda " ? " (tanya) diakhir kalimat nya)
