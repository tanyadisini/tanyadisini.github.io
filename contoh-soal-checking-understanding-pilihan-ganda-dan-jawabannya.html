Dimas : Hi Ton, what’s up? You look confused. (Hai Ton, ada apa? Kamu terlihat bingung).

Toni : Yeah, actually I have a problem when I wanted to install the game that you gave, it couldn’t be installed. (Ya, sebenarnya aku memiliki masalah ketika aku ingin menginstal permainan yang kamu berikan, permainannya tidak bisa diinstal).

Dimas : Oh, I see. Have you followed the instructions correctly? (Oh, begitu. Sudahkah kamu mengikuti instruksi – instruksinya dengan benar?).

Toni : Yeah, I think so. I have followed the instructions one by one. However, at the end it

couldn’t be played. I thought something was wrong. (Ya, aku rasa begitu. Aku sudah mengikuti instruksi – instruksinya satu per satu. Akan tetapi, pada akhirnya permainannya tidak bisa dimainkan. Aku pikir ada sesuatu yang salah).

Dimas : Let me check for a while Ton. Please hand me your laptop. (Coba aku periksa sebentar Ton. Berikan aku laptopmu).

Toni : Sure, here it is. (Tentu, ini laptopku).

Dimas : Yap, I got it. Here is the problem Ton. (Yap, aku menemukannya. Ini masalahnya Ton).

Toni : What’s the problem? (Apa masalahnya?).

Dimas : Before you install the game, you need to turn off your antivirus as some files on the game will be deleted automatically when the game is installed. So, make sure you turn it off first and then you can install the game. Do you understand what I’m saying Ton? (Sebelum kamu menginstal permainannya, kamu perlu mematikan anti virusmu karena beberapa file pada permainan akan dihapus secara otomatis ketika gamenya diisntal. Jadi, pastikan kamu mematikannya terlebih dulu lalu kamu bisa menginstal permainannya. Apakah kamu mengerti apa yang aku katakan Ton?).

Toni : Yeah, I understand what you are saying. I don’t know the antivirus will affect the installation process. (Ya, aku paham dengan apa yang kamu katakan. Aku tidak tahu anti virusnya akan mempengaruhi proses instalasinya).

Dimas : I’m sorry Ton, that was my mistake because I didn’t tell you before. (Maaf Ton, itu kesalahanku karena aku tidak memberitahumu sebelumnya).

Toni : That’s okay Dimas. (Tidak apa-apa Dimas).

Dimas : Now, let’s try to install the game so we can play together then. (Sekarang, ayo kita coba instal permainannya jadi kita bisa bermain bersama).

Toni : Okay, let’s do it. (Baik, ayo kita lakukan).

(Ya, tetapi saya masih belum yakin dengan itu. Bisakah kamu mengajari saya?)

(Ya tentu. Haruskah kita belajar tentang itu di perpustakaan sekolah?)

(Ya tentu, ayo pergi sekarang.)

25. contoh dialog checking understanding


Berikut ini adalah contoh dialog tentang checking understanding

Xavier: Abdul, I wonder if you could help me out.

Abdul: Sure, mate, what's up?

Xavier: It's about the Cambridge English test. I don't really get it.

Abdul: Oh, ok, that happens to be my expertise. What test will you take?

Xavier: FCE (First Certificate of English)

Abdul: OK, FCE is one of the mid high-level of English exams, precisely just two more levels to the top level, CPE (Cambridge Proficiency English). You know, unlike TOEFL or IELTS, in Cambridge system, there are levels in its English exams, like five levels for adult general English. FCE is on level 3. The order is KET, PET, FCE, CAE and CPE. Each is more challenging than the previous one.

Xavier: I see, hmm, so is it like TOEFL then? I mean the format.

Abdul: The format is not exactly the same, but the skill to be tested is similar, you know, speaking, listening, writing, reading and grammar. Do you get it?

Xavier: I get it now.

Abdul:Oh, perhaps one major difference than the exams is the speaking.

Xavier: What about it?

Abdul: you have to do it in pairs, directly in front of two native speakers.

Xavier: Oh really? that's quite daunting then.

Abdul: No, it's fine. Oh, the listening is also in British English, sometimes with kinda strong accent, you know. So, do you understand it now?

Xavier: Yeah, man, thanks.

Abdul: Just log on to its website for more info.

Xavier: Sure, I will.

Abdul: Good luck for the test then.

Xavier: Thanks, mate.

39. apakah contoh dialog checking for understanding?


“Checking for understanding” adalah kalimat – kalimat yang bermaksud mengecek apakah lawan bicara sudah mengerti tujuan dan maksud kita. Contoh dialog “Checking for understanding” adalah sebagai berikut:


A: Hello, can you help me to find the way to go to X?

B: Sure. Are you taking the bus or on foot?

A: I will go by bus.

B: Ok, so from here, you can take bus number 601 in front of this building. And then you will transfer to bus 255 at M Station.

A: Yes. And after that? Where should I get off?

B: The final stop of bus 255 is N Station. You can get off there and walk about 15 minutes to X. Have you got it? Any question?

A: Yes, I got it. Thank you for your help.


Kalimat – kalimat yang dapat digunakan untuk “checking for understanding” antara lain:

1. Do you know what I mean?

2. Do you know what I'm saying?

3. Do you understand?

4. Are you following me?

5. Are you with me (so far)?

6. Have you got it?

7. Any questions?
