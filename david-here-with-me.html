31. Listen to a radio interview with David McLain, an explorer and journalist. Answer the questions. 1. What does David want to know? 2. Why is he in Sardinia? ​


Understanding Context

Jawaban:

In order to answer the questions below, we need to read the interview between the presenter and David McLain first. Some answers may be stated clearly in the text.

1. What does David want to know?

Answer: From the presenter introduction we know that David wants to know the reasons why some people live longer than others. He has many questions in his mind like: what the reason why some people live longer, is it because of their diet or their lifestyle?or other reasons?

2. Why is he in Sardinia?

Answer: The reason why David is in Sardinia at that time was explained in the first question of the interview. He is in Sardinia to find out the reason why some people live longer. Sardinia is an interesting place, as in that city men live the same amount of time as women, and that also an interesting fact because in most countries men normally die younger.
