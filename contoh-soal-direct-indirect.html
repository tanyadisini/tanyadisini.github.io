3. Pengertian Direct And Indirect Rumus Direct And Indirect Contoh Direct And Indirect


- direct speech merupakan cara untuk melaporkan apa yang orang telah katakan atau tulis berupa pernyataan, pertanyaan, atau ucapan lainnya dengan mengutip persis perkataan nya.  sebaliknya pada indirect speech adalah perkataan disampaikan dengan mengubah format nya agar menjadi lebih jelas, alami, dan efisien bagi pendengar nya.
- "I'll be back soon," he said ( direct speech )
  He said that he would be back soon ( indirect speech )

4. contoh dari direct dan indirect?


Direct speech
Berupa statement : “I’m not hungry now.”
(Saya tidak lapar sekarang)
Imperative : “Do not touch my computer!”
(Jangan sentuh komputerku!)
Yes-no question : “Did you eat my cake?”
(Apakah kamu makan kueku?)
Information request : “Who is the winner?” (Siapa pemenangnya?)

Indirect speech
Statement : He said that he was not hungry at that time.
Imperative : She told me not to touch her computer.
Yes-no question : She wanted to know if I’d ate her cake.
Information request : He asked me who the winner was.
Direct Speech : Berbentuk kalimat langsung
"I did not do that thing"
Indirect : Berbentuk pernyataan
She said that she didn't do that thing

19. 5 contoh kalimat direct dan indirect yang jelas

direct : Verajun told me, “I go to work everyday.”

indirect : she told me that she went to work everyday.

Present Continuous menjadi Past Continuous

direct : Verajun told me, “I am playing music now”.

indirect : she told me that she was playing music then.


Present Perfect menjadi Past Perfect

direct : They told me. “We have bought a car”.

indirect : They told me that they had bought a car.


Past tense menjadi Past Perfect

direct : Verajun told me, “I went to Japan yesterday”.

indirect : she told me that she had gone to Japan yesterday.


Simple Future menjadi Future Past

direct : Verajun told me, “I will go to Singapore next year”.

indirect : She told me that he would go to Singapore the following year.

20. contoh kalimat negatif direct dan indirect​


Contoh kalimat negatif dalam bentuk direct (langsung):

1. "I don't like spicy food." (Saya tidak suka makanan pedas.)
2. "She isn't coming to the party." (Dia tidak datang ke pesta.)
3. "They didn't pass the exam." (Mereka tidak lulus ujian.)
4. "He doesn't want to go on vacation." (Dia tidak ingin pergi berlibur.)
5. "We haven't finished the project yet." (Kami belum menyelesaikan proyek itu.)

Contoh kalimat negatif dalam bentuk indirect (tidak langsung):

1. She told me that she didn't like spicy food. (Dia memberitahu saya bahwa dia tidak suka makanan pedas.)
2. They informed us that they weren't coming to the party. (Mereka memberitahu kami bahwa mereka tidak akan datang ke pesta.)
3. He mentioned that they didn't pass the exam. (Dia menyebutkan bahwa mereka tidak lulus ujian.)
4. My friend said that he didn't want to go on vacation. (Teman saya mengatakan bahwa dia tidak ingin pergi berlibur.)
5. The team leader mentioned that they hadn't finished the project yet. (Pemimpin tim menyebutkan bahwa mereka belum menyelesaikan proyek itu.)

Perhatikan bahwa kalimat-kalimat negatif ini menyatakan ketidaksetujuan, penolakan, atau kegagalan dalam hal tertentu.
