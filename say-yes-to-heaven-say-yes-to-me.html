22. keterangan tentang to greet,to take leave,to say thank you,to say sorry


keterangan gimana maksudnya? atau mungkin pernyataan untuk menyatakn gitu?
-to greeting : untuk memberikan ucapan selamat dari suatu pencapaian seseorang. misalnya : congratulation
-to take leave : untuk meninggalkan. misalnya dengan pernyataan "i have to go" dan memberi ucapan "good bye"
-to say thank you : untuk menyatakan terimakasih. biasa menggunakan kata " thanks/ thank you"
-to say sorry : untuk menyatakan minta maaf. dengan mengatakan "forgive" atau "im sorry" yang didasari rasa penyesalan.

30. -beside is used to say?-around is used to say?


Beside is used to say when you want to describe something which is close, or nearby. Around is used to say when you want to describe something which is located surrounding a certain area.


Pembahasan

1. Beside: at the side of, next to

Contoh kalimat:

He was sitting beside me in the train.I always put my glasses beside the lamp.

2. Around: on every side of, surrounding

Contoh kalimat:

I would like to take a walk around the park.Look! Her clothes are all around the corner of my room.
