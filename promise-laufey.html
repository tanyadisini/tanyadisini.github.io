20. respon negatif postif expressing promise


Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: promise
Pembahasan:

In expression of promise, there are, as usual, two kinds of response, the positive and negative ones. Here are the examples:

R: Sweetie, I promise I'll never leave you. I'll always take care of you till the end of time.
P (positive response): Really?!? Oh, how sweet of you! I'm so lucky to have you. I'll do the same to you.

N (negative response): Really! Those words again. Yeah, you keep telling yourself that as if they believe in you. Do you hear yourself? It's the same words over and over again without any proof.

29. He wishes he hadn't forgotten his promise. This means that ______ A. he tried not to forget his promise B. he did not forget his promise C. he forgets his promise D. he forgot his promise E. he could not forget his promise


Jawaban:

The correct answer for the question is as follows.

d. He forgot his promise.

The answer should use Simple Past Tense as the question use Simple Present Tense.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban yang tepat haruslah menggunakan bentuk Simple Past Tense. Hal ini karena tindakan lupa disadari oleh pneuturnya. Artinya, beberapa saat yang lalu, ia lupa namun saat ini ia sadar bahwa telah melupakan sesuatu.
