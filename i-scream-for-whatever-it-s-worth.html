13. Quizzz Soal:→ 6³ - 4⁵ + 8⁴ = ?→ 8² × 5³ - 6³ = ?Note:- Whatever I will do for you Cinthya -​


1.) Diketahui :

6³ - 4⁵ + 8⁴

Ditanya :

Hasil ?

Jawab :

= 6³ - 4⁵ + 8⁴

= ( 6 × 6 × 6 ) - ( 4 × 4 × 4 × 4 × 4 ) + ( 8 × 8 × 8 × 8 )

= 216 - 1.024 + 4.096

= -808 + 4.096

= 3.288

________________________

2.) Diketahui :

8² × 5³ - 6³

Ditanya :

Hasil ?

Jawab :

= 8² × 5³ - 6³

= ( ( 8 × 8 ) × ( 5 × 5 × 5 ) ) - ( 6 × 6 × 6 )

= ( 64 × 125 ) - 216

= 8.000 - 216

= 7.784

Kesimpulan :

1.) 3.288

2.) 7.784

Jawaban:

1. 3.288

2. 7.784
