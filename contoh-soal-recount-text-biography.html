Biografi Muhammad Hatta

Mohammad Hatta or often called Bung Hatta was the first Vice President of Indonesia. Mohammad Hatta was born in Bukit tinggi, West Sumatera on August 12th, 1902. His father is Haji Mohammad Djamil and his mother is Siti Saleha. His father died when he was eight months old. Hatta married to Rahmi Hatta on November 18th, 1945. The couple had three children named Meutia Farida Hatta, Gemala Rabi’ah Hatta, and Halida Nuriah Hatta.

Hatta started his education at a private school named Sekolah Melayu. Then he went to ELS (European Language School). He continued his school to MULO (Meer Uitgebreid Lager Onderwijs). Hatta began to show his interested in politics and national movement since he was sixteen years old. He joined Jong Sumatranen Bond and he was chosen as the treasurer.

In 1919, Hatta went to Hogere Burgerschool (HBS) in Batavia (Jakarta). He finished his study with distinction in 1921 and he was allowed to continue his study to Rotterdam School of Commerce in Netherlands. He took economics as his major and got a doctorandus degree. He then continued to pursue his doctorate degree, but he didn’t finish his thesis because politics had taken over his life.

In Netherlands Hatta joined the Indische Vereeniging. In 1922, Indische Vereeniging changed its named to Indonesische Vereeniging (Perhimpoenan Indonesia). Hatta was the treasurer from 1922-1925 and then he became the chairman from 1926-1930. Perhimpoenan Indonesia then changed from a student organization to political organization that demand for Indonesia’s Independence. It expressed its voice through a magazine called Indonesia Merdeka of which Hatta was the editor.

Hatta attended congresses all over Europe to gain more support from other nations, he always as the chairman of Indonesia delegation. By the middle of 1927, Perhimpoenan Indonesia’s activites had alarmed the Dutch authorities. On June 1927, Dutch authorities put Hatta and four other Indonesian activists in jail. In 1929, Hatta and other Perhimpoenan Indonesia activists were released.

TAMBAHAN MATERI TENTANG HISTORICAL RECOUNT TEXT

Pergertiaan historical recount

text Historical recount is a form of recount text whose contents tell historical events. In short, historical recount is a historical story in English. Like the form of recount text in general, historical recount uses the simple past tense in its writing.

Language feature in recount text

Using chronological connections such as first, then and so on. Using linking verbs such as were, was, heard, saw and so on. Using action verbs such as go, look, change, run and so on. Using the simple past tense.

struktur historical recount text

The generic structure of the recount text is: Orientation: introduces the character, time or place. Events: various events that are told sequentially (in chronological order). Re-orientation: the end of a story, can be the author's impression or comment on the story.

11. Perbedaan dan persamaan recount text and biography


Perbedaan dan persamaan recount text dan biography:

Perbedaan:

- Recount text menceritakan pengalaman yang dialami oleh seseorang, sedangkan biography menceritakan riwayat hidup seseorang (umumnya orang yang terkenal).

- Recount text berfokus pada 1 kejadian dalam hidup seseorang, sedangkan biography berfokus pada banyak kejadian (khususnya kejadian penting) yang terjadi sepanjang hidup seseorang.

Persamaan:

- Recount text dan biography sama-sama menceritakan kejadian di masa lampau.

- Recount text dan biography sama-sama menggunakan past tense.

- Susunan teks terdiri atas orientation, events, dan re-orientation.

Penjelasan:

Recount Text

Recount text adalah jenis teks dalam Bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk menceritakan suatu kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau.

Social Function

Recount text berfungsi untuk menghibur serta menginformasikan pembaca mengenai pengalaman penulis.

Generic Structure

1. Orientation, bagian ini berisi pembuka mengenai latar belakang terjadinya suatu kejadian, baik waktu, tempat, serta orang yang terlibat.

2. Events, berisi rangkaian peristiwa terjadinya suatu kejadian yang disusun secara kronologis.

3. Re-orientation, bagian ini berisi penutup serta kesimpulan dari seluruh kejadian.

Biography

Biography adalah jenis teks yang digunakan untuk menceritakan tentang riwayat hidup atau perjalanan hidup seseorang. Umumnya, biography hanya ditujukan untuk menceritakan tentang riwayat hidup orang yang terkenal.

Social Function

Biography berfungsi untuk menginformasikan pembaca mengenai perjalanan hidup seseorang, kesulitan yang dihadapi dan usaha yang dilakukan hingga dapat dikenal.

Generic Structure

1. Orientation, bagian ini berisi pengenalan tokoh, seperti tempat dan tanggal lahir serta latar belakang keluarg.

2. Events, berisi rangkaian peristiwa-peristiwa penting yang terjadi dalam hidup seseorang yang disusun secara kronologis, seperti proses berkaris, masalah yang dihadapi, dan bagaimana ia menyelesaikan masalah tersebut.

3. Re-orientation, bagian ini berisi penutup serta kesimpulan dari seluruh kejadian. Pada bagian ini, penulis biasanya memberikan pendapat pribadinya mengenai tokoh yang diceritakan.
