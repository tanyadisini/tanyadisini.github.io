32. apa itu past continuous dan contohnya (kalo bisa yang panjang pengertiannya soalnya untuk tugas)


Past continous tense atau past progressive tense adalah suatu bentuk kata kerja yang digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa suatu aksi sedang terjadi pada waktu tertentu di masa lampau. contoh, My family and I were going to zoo yesterday.

semoga membantu,jawaban tercerdas yaapast continuous is a tense talk about action that was happening around a particular time in the past. the formula : subject +was/were + Verb-ing
e.g.:
I was speaking English.
sometimes, we use the past continuous and the past simple tense together. e.g :
she was listening the to the radio when I came.
they were watching television when their mother came in.
