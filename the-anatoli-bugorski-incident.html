40. What is the inciting incident or destabilizing event


The inciting incident of a story is the event that sets the main character or characters on the journey that will occupy them throughout the narrative. Typically, this incident will upset the balance within the main character’s world. In moments big and small, an inciting incident changes the life of a character, and the ensuing story is the fallout from that change.
