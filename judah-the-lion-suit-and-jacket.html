6. rangkuman cerita The Lion the wish and The wardrobe​


Jawaban:

The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe is a children's fantasy novel by C.S. Lewis. The story follows four siblings, Peter, Susan, Edmund, and Lucy, who are sent to live in the countryside during World War II. Lucy discovers a magical wardrobe that leads to the land of Narnia, where she meets a faun named Mr. Tumnus and learns that Narnia is ruled by an evil White Witch. Edmund follows Lucy into Narnia and is tempted by the Witch's promises of power and Turkish Delight. The siblings eventually unite with Aslan, a lion who is the true ruler of Narnia, and fight against the Witch's army. Aslan sacrifices himself to save Edmund, but is later resurrected and defeats the Witch. The siblings are crowned as kings and queens of Narnia and eventually return to their own world. The novel is known for its Christian allegory and use of classical mythology.

"The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe" adalah sebuah novel fantasi yang ditulis oleh C.S. Lewis dan merupakan buku pertama dalam seri "The Chronicles of Narnia". Berikut adalah ringkasan singkat ceritanya:

Cerita ini berkisah tentang empat saudara: Peter, Susan, Edmund, dan Lucy. Mereka dievakuasi dari London selama Perang Dunia II dan dikirim untuk tinggal di sebuah mansion di pedesaan yang dimiliki oleh seorang profesor tua. Saat menjelajahi mansion tersebut, Lucy tanpa sengaja menemukan lemari pakaian ajaib yang mengantarnya ke negeri Narnia, sebuah dunia ajaib yang penuh dengan hewan-hewan yang bisa berbicara dan makhluk mitos.

Lucy berteman dengan seorang faun bernama Mr. Tumnus, yang menceritakan kepadanya tentang kondisi Narnia saat ini. Narnia dikuasai oleh Ratu Putih jahat, yang telah menjatuhkan negeri itu ke dalam musim dingin abadi tetapi tanpa perayaan Natal. Lucy kembali ke dunia nyata, tetapi saudara-saudaranya tidak percaya pada ceritanya.

Akhirnya, keempat saudara tersebut masuk ke Narnia melalui lemari pakaian. Mereka mengetahui tentang sebuah ramalan yang mengatakan bahwa dua anak laki-laki dari Adam dan dua anak perempuan dari Hawa akan membebaskan Narnia dari tirani Ratu Putih. Bersama dengan bantuan seekor singa bernama Aslan, mereka memulai sebuah pencarian untuk mengalahkan Ratu Putih dan mengembalikan perdamaian ke Narnia.

Cerita ini adalah kisah petualangan klasik tentang keberanian dan pertempuran antara kebaikan dan kejahatan dalam dunia ajaib yang tersembunyi di balik sebuah lemari pakaian biasa.

One day a lion was resting in the forest. He was sleeping and leaning his body under a tree. Then a mouse ran toward the lion without seeing him asleep in front of him. He was bumping the lion’s face so hard that made him awake.

The lion woke up and mad at him, “Woaahhh, how dare you disturb my sleep?” Then the lion caught the mouse. “I’m sorry my king! I accidentally hit you! “He said. “I will eat you because you have wakened me up!” cried the lion.

“Please! Do not eat me my king; I will reply your kindness someday if you let me go!“ Beg the mouse. Hearing what was said by the mouse, the lion laugh out loud, “HHaaaa you will repay my kindness? How can a little creature like you can help me!” He said. Then He looked at the mouse who was frightened. “Well since you’ve made me laugh, I’ll let you go. Moreover, you cannot make me satisfied “said the Lion while releasing the mouse.

After he let him go, the mouse ran and said, “I will not forget your kindness. Thank you “. Then he was back into the forest. One day the lion was caught by hunter’s trap. He was trapped in a net installed by hunter. The lion was struggling trying to escape from the net. But all of his efforts were useless because the net was too strong. Then he was screaming for help “Please, save me!” the lion cried.

The mouse that was foraging in the forest heard lion’ screams. “That is lion. Seemed he is in trouble now. I have to help him “said the mouse. Soon afterward he went to lion. When he got there, he saw a lion that had trapped helpless in hunter’s nets. “Hey, do not be afraid, I’ll help you escape from this bondage” said the mouse. “What? You want to save me? Don’t be kidding “said the lion. Then the mouse was climbing to the top of the net and began biting rope nets one by one with his sharp teeth. The lion, who initially did not believe, surprised to see what he did.

After a while the ropes broken and the lion could get out of the entanglement nets, “Thank you! My little friend, you have freed me. I do not think you can do it “said the lion. “You are welcome my friend. I had promised to repay your kindness someday so I did it “replied the mouse. After that they were always together and became good friends in the forest.

Kisah Si Singa dan Si Tikus

Pada suatu hari seekor singa sedang beristirahat di hutan. Hia sedang tertidur dengan menyandarkan tubuhnya di bawah pohon yang besar. Kemudian seekor tikus berlari ke arah singa itu tanpa melihat dia sedang tertidur di depannya. Tikus tersebut menabrak wajah singa itu dengan keras sehingga membuatnya terbangun dari tidur.

Singa itu terbangun dan marah kepadanya, “Woaahhh, kau berani menggangu tidur siaangku?”. Lalu singa itu menangkap tikus tersebut. “Maafkan aku raja! aku tidak sengaja menabrakmu!” kata tikus itu. “Kau akan ku makan karena telah membangunkanku!” Teriak singa itu.

“Ampun! jangan makan aku raja, aku akan membalas kebaikanmu suatu hari nanti jika kau melepaskanku!” mohon tikus itu. Mendengar apa yang dikatakan oleh tikus itu, Sang singa tertawa dengan keras, “HHaaaa kau akan membalas kebaikanku? bagaiman bisa kau makhluk kecil menolongku! hhaa!” kata singa itu. Dia pun memandangi tikus yang sedang ketakutan itu. “Baiklah karena kau sudah membuatku tertawa, aku akan melepaskanmu. Lagipula kau tidak bisa membuat aku kenyang” kata sang singa sambil melepaskan tikus itu.

Setelah dia melepaskan tikus itu, Sang tikus berlari dan mengatakan,”Aku tidak akan melupakan kebaikanmu. Terimakasih”. Tikus pun kembali kedalam hutan. Pada suatu hari sang singa tertangkap oleh jebakan pemburu. Dia terjebak di dalam sebuah jaring yang dipasang oleh pemburu. Singa itu meronta-ronta berusaha untuk lepas dari jaring itu. Namun semua usahanya sia-sia karena jaring itu terlalu kuat. Sang singa kemudian menjerit untuk meminta tolong “Tolong, selamatkan aku!” teriak singa itu.

Kebetulan tikus yang sedang mencari makan di sekitar hutan mendengar suara jeritan singa. “Sepertinya itu suara singa.Dia terdengar sedang dalam kesulitan. Aku harus membantunya” kata tikus itu. Kemudian tikus itu segera pergi ke arah singa. Sesampainya di sana, dia melihat singa yang sudah tidak berdaya terperangkap jaring pemburu. “Hey singa jangan takut, aka akan membantumu lepas dari jebakan ini” kata tikus itu. “Apa? kau ingin menyelamatkanku? Jangan bercanda” kata singa itu. Lalu tikus itu memanjat ke atas jaring dan mulai menggigiti tali jaring satu persatu dengan giginya yang tajam. Sang singa yang awalnya tidak mempercayainya terkejut melihat apa yang dilakukan oleh tikus.

Setelah beberapa lama tali-tali itu putus dan singa bisa keluar dari jebakan jaring, “Terimaksih kawan kecilku, kau telah membebaskanku. Aku tidak menyangka kau bisa melakukanya” kata singa itu. “Sama-sama teman. Aku telah berjanji untuk membalas kebaikanmu suatu hari nanti jadi aku melakukanya” jawab tikus. Setelah kejadian itu mereka selalu bersama dan menjadi teman baik di dalam hutan.

A lion was getting fed up of hunting. He called all animals and passed an order, "Everyday one of you should come by yourself as my prey". All animals started to obey this order. 

It was the hare's day. The hare was very sad. As he was going to the lion's den, he came across an old well. He looked into it. It was very deep and dangerous. He made a plan to his mind. 

The lion was very angry that no animal had come that day. The hare slowly came towards the lion. The lion roared "Why are you late?" The hare humbly replied, "Oh my way another lion chased me. I got away with much difficulty to give my life to you, Your Majesty". 

The lion was pleased with the hare. But the thought of another lion in the jungle made him angry. The lion roared "Do you know where he lives?" The hare replied "Yes, Sir. Please come with me". 

The hare took the lion to the old well. He said "Sir, that lion lives in this well". The lion peeped into the well. He mistook his reflection for another lion. He roared and there was an echo. He thought that the other lion was roaring too and jumped into the well. That was the end of the lion.

36. Deskripsi lion and the mouse


The Lion And The Mouse
Once when a Lion was asleep, a little Mouse began running up and down upon him. This soon wakened the Lion, who placed his huge paw upon him and opened his big jaws to swallow him.
"Pardon, O King!" cried the little Mouse, "Forgive me this time. I shall never repeat it and I shall never forget your kindness. And who knows, but I may be able to do you a good turn one of these days?"
The Lion was so tickled at the idea of the Mouse being able to help him, that he lifted up his paw and let him go.
Sometime later a few hunters captured the King and tied him to a tree while they went in search of a wagon to carry him on.
Just then the little Mouse happened to pass by, and seeing the sad plight in which the Lion was, ran up to him and soon gnawed away the ropes that bound the King of the Beasts. "Was I not right?" said the little Mouse, very happy to help the lion.
