Jawaban:

+)My parents have been married for 25 years. *

?) Have my parents been married for 25 years?

Penjelasan:

Materi: Present perfect tense

Rumus :

+) S + Have/ has + V3

-) S + Have/Has + not + V3

?) Have/has + S + V3

~Tense

Form of Present Perfect Tense :

S + have / has + Past Participle + Obj / Adv

(+) My parents have been married for 25 years

(-) My parents have not been married for 25 years

(?) Have My parents been married for 25 years ?
