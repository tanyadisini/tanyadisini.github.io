15. buatlah percakapan 4 orang tentang expressing certainty and uncertainty


kelas : VII
pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
kategori : Expression
kata kunci : certainty, uncertainty, expression

pembahasan:
certainty expressions adalah ungkapan-ungkapan yang menunjukkan kepastian, sedangkan uncertainty expressions adalah ungkapan-yngkapan yang menunjukkan ketidakpastian atau keraguan akan sesuatu

contoh dialog  untuk empat orang
Your teacher : Good morning, students.
Students : Good morning, Ma'am.
Your teacher : Today, I will take score for tenses. Who didn't come to the class?
Student 1 : Nesye, Ma'am.
Your teacher : Did she give a permission letter?
Student 2 : No, she didn't, Ma'am. But she told us why she didn't come to school today.
Your teacher : What happened with her?
Student 3 : She told that her father hospitalized two days ago, Ma'am.
Your teacher : Are you sure? (Asking for certainty)
Student 4 : I'm not sure, Ma'am. Because I saw her father near my house yesterday, Ma'am. (uncertainty expression)
Student 2 : I doubt that too, Ma'am. I think she wasn't ready to take English score. She told me that she didn't understand this chapter, Ma'am. (uncertainty expression)
Student 3 : I’ve no doubt about that information, because she came to my house to tell that, Ma'am. (certainty expression)
Student 1 : I’m certain about that, Ma'am. (Certainty expression)
Your teacher : Okay. Maybe I should call her parents after class. Thank you for your information. Are you ready for the test?
Students : Yes, Ma'am.
