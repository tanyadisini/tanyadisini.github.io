38. which type of business allows the owner to keep all of the profit for him/herself


Businnes that allows the owner ko keep all of the profit for him or herself is Sole proprietorship. Sole Proprietorship is a bussiness that owned by one person only.

Pembahasan:

Theres five business ownership type: Sole Proprietorship: this type of business have no distinction between the business owner's personal and professional assets. This type of busniess is hard ro raise money but have lower startup cost. Partenrship: type of business that similliar with sole proprietorship but partership us owned by two or more people. In this type of business owner have limited partership and limited liability partnerships. The profits will be devided by their owner. Limited liability company: This type of business separates between personal and business assets. Personal money prom the owner will be save if someday the business is bankrupt.Corporations: The asset between personal and business are devided, funding for business is from stock selling, theres double taxation. Cooperative: business owner and operated by the same people that use its product and service. The profit will be devided among the people who work with the cooperative.
