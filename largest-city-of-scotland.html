5. Jelaskan apa itu city of tomorrow, city of sorrow, metropolis, dan miseropolis


MISEROPOLIS : merupakan terminologi yang dipakai untuk menggambarkan kondisi suatu kota yang pengap, kotor, acak-acakan, melarat, tercemar dan menyedihkan, atau secara leksikal mengandung arti Kota yang Sakit (Ghetto)

METROPOLIS berasal dan bahasa Yunani, yaitu dari kata meter yang bermakna ‘ibu’ dan polis bermakna (1) ‘ibu kota’ atau ‘kota terpenting dalam negara atau wilayah’ dan (2) ‘kota yang menjadi pusat kegiatan perdagangan industri, dan pemerintahan.

CITY OF TOMORROW menggambarkan kota masa depan. Dimana di kota tersebut menggambarkan keteraturan, kenyamanan, kebahagiaan dan modernisasi.

CITY OF SORROW (sorrow artinya kesedihan) merupakan kota yang menggambarkan kesedihan dan kesemerawutan dan jauh dari kenyamanan.

22. 12 1. Hanif has seven data of natural numbers. The average of the data is 50. The difference between the largest d he number of the largest of possible data is.​


Let's assume the largest data in Hanif's set of natural numbers is "L". Since the average of the data is 50, we can write the following equation:

(First data + Second data + ... + L) / 7 = 50

Since we know that there are seven data points in total, we can rewrite the equation as:

(First data + Second data + ... + L) = 7 * 50

Simplifying further:

First data + Second data + ... + L = 350

Now, we need to find the maximum possible value for L that satisfies this equation. Since we want to maximize L, we can assume that the remaining six data points are all equal to 1 (the smallest possible natural number).

So, the equation becomes:

1 + 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 + L = 350

6 + L = 350

L = 350 - 6

L = 344

Therefore, the largest possible data value, L, is 344.

25. 2. The largest prime factor of 330 is​


Jawaban:

To find the largest prime factor of 330, we can start by dividing it by the smallest prime number, which is 2.

330 divided by 2 is 165.

Next, we continue dividing the result by prime numbers until we can no longer divide evenly.

165 divided by 3 is 55.

55 divided by 5 is 11.

11 is a prime number, and it cannot be divided further.

Therefore, the largest prime factor of 330 is 11.
