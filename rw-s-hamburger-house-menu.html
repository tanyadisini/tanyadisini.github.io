21. Kak buatin procedure teks tentang hamburger.


Ingredients:
1. 10 slices of burger bread, ready to use
2. 10 sheets of lettuce
3. 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
4. 2 tomatoes, thinly sliced
5. 10 sheets of cheese
6. Mayonnaise
7. Tomato sauce
8. Chilly sauce
9. 300 grams of minced beef
10. ½ onion, finely chopped
11. 4 cloves of gralic, finely chopped
12. salt and pepper
How to cook the beef:
1. Take a medium-sized bowl and then put minced beef, salt, pepper powder, garlic and onions that have been chopped, and stir it until mixed well.
2. After all of the ingredients are mixed well, take 1 or 2 tablespoon of the beef mixture and shape it into flat round by your hands. Do this step continuously.
3. Take the pan and heat the butter by using a small fire and wait until it is hot enough. After that, enter the flat round beef mixture into the pan and cook it.
How to serve the burger:
1. After you fry all of the beef on the pan, do this suggestion to serve the burger.
2. Take a slice of bread then slice it into two.
3. After that, butter the surface of the bread, then put its surface on the hot pan for about 30 seconds.
4. Next, take the bread and put mayonnaise, tomato sauce, and chilly sauce on the surface of the bread.
5. Then, arrange lettuce, beef, cheese, tomato, cucumber, and onions sufficiently.
6. Do those steps to all of the ingredients.
7. Finally, the burgers are ready to serve.

35. Ada 1 orang membeli 4949 hamburger di restoran, kemudian orang itu memberikan kepada 7 teman dekat dengan jumlah yang sama banyak, berapa hamburger yang diberikan kepada 7 teman-temannya tersebut?​


Seseorang membeli 4949 hamburger di sebuah restoran dan membagikan kepada 7 temannya sama banyak, berapa hamburger yang diberikan...?

Jumlah burger yang diberikan :

[tex] = \frac{4949}{7} \\ = 707 \: hamburger[/tex]

Jadi, tiap orang temannya mendapatkan 707 hamburger
