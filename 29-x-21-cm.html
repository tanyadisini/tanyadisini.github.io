[tex]\bf\large{C.\ 3,5×10¹\ Newton} [/tex]

Penjelasan:

Diketahui;

[tex]\sf\large{Q_{1}=7×10^{-4}\ C }[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{Q₂ =2×10^{-6}\ C }[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{r = 60\ cm = 6×10^{-1}\ m }[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{k = 9×10⁹\ Nm²/C² }[/tex]

Jawab;

Gunakan rumus;

[tex]\sf\large{F.r² = k.Q_{1}.Q₂ }[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{F} = \LARGE{\frac{k.Q_{1}.Q₂}{r²} }[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{F} =\LARGE{\frac{9×10⁹.7×10^{-4}.2×10^{-6}}{(6×10^{-1})²} }[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{F} =\LARGE{\frac{9.7.2×10^{9-4-6-(-2)}}{36}=\frac{126}{36}}\large{×10¹ }[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{F = 3,5×10¹\ N }[/tex]

Jadi gaya Coulomb-nya sebesar [tex]\sf\large3,5×10¹\ Newton [/tex]
