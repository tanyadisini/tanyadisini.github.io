9. Bagaimana menjawab jika seseorang bilang see you soon


The expression 'see you soon' is one of the leave-takings one. Such is uttered before parting to each other. The proper response for 'see you soon' is as follows:

see you see you too

Pembahasan:

The expression of leave-takings such as 'see you soon' is one of the expression of farewell or partings. There are other similar expressions for such situation such as

see you later goodbye byesee you around see yaso long farewell bye bye

The only difference in using those expressions is the register (formal or informal) or in layman's term, the kind of situation suitable for those words.

Meanwhile, the way to respond those expressions is mainly repeating them instead of making new expressions.

17. apa artinya all right , see you soon


Arti all right, see you soon adalah baiklah, sampai jumpa.PembahasanFarewell Greetings

Ucapan perpisahan diucapkan saat kita hendak berpisah dari orang lain. Kata-kata dapat dirangkai berdasarkan variasi waktu perpisahan dengan orang tersebut. Makna dari pengucapan ini adalah sebagai salam dan doa.

Ditanyakan

Apa artinya "all right, see you soon".

Penjelasan

Arti all right, see you soon adalah baiklah, sampai jumpa.

All right = baiklahsee you = sampai jumpasoon = segera

Kalimat ini diucapkan setelah terjadi sebuah percakapan antara pihak-pihak tersebut. Kata "soon" menunjukkan bahwa mereka belum tentu bertemu lagi dalam waktu singkat meskipun hanya sementara (temporary).

Contoh lainI hope I will see you again soon. (Aku harap aku dapat bertemu lagi denganmu segera).Have a nice day, see you later.
