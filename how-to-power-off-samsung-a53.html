17. Bagaimana Cara Power Off Dengan Keyboard Di PC


alt + f4, tapi ada juga laptop yang pake alt + fn + f4.1.Tekan Tombol Logo Windows , maka akan muncul taskbar start, kemudian geser menggunakan Panah atas / bawah / kanan / kiri hingga terpilih menu shutdown kemudian ENTER…
2.Selain itu, tekan CTRL + ESC secara berbarengan untuk memunculkan Taskbar Start kemudian arahkan menggunakan Arah Panah di Keybord hingga menunjuk pada bagian menu Shutdown, lalu Enter
3.Biasa nya ada keyboard yang telah disediakan Button Off (shutdown) , jika ada maka kamu bisa tekan tombol power off di Keyboard, maka fungsinya sama dengan menu shutdown.
4.Cara alternative terakhir yaitu menggunakan cara cepat , dengan menekan ALT + F4 , maka akan muncul Menu Shutdown, dan kemudian ENTER.

18. how did stalin rise to power and defeat trotsky


Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: Sejarah
Kategori: biografi tokoh
Pembahasan:

Stalin was able to rise as Lenin's successor and defeat his rival, Trotsky despite being less intelligent than him because of several factors such as:
- Stalin's pragmatic nature to make his ambition come true by taking any chances possible without considering idealism, something that Trostky clung on firmly.
- Stalin didn't mind building coalition with anyone, as long as it could lead to his ambition.
- Stalin's ability to manipulate the public in order to get their sympathy, such as naming the city Petrograd as Leningrad to gain more support from the people.
- A number of Trostsky's fatal mistake, which led to his failure to be Lenin's successor, such as, not coming to Lenin's funeral, attacking people who were the key of his plan, criticising Lenin in his personal exposed letter to his friend, despite a great recommendation from Lenin to himself as the candidate successor.

Such unfortunate events on Trotsky and Stalin's ability to turn things round for his own advantage were the key of Stalin's success to replace Lenin.

Shut down - shuts down the computer, and installs any Windows Updates.
Restart - shuts down the computer, installs any Windows Updates, and starts your computer again. Useful if your computer is having trouble with hardware and programs.
Sleep - turns off your computer's display and temporarily halts any running programs.
Hibernate - turns off your computer and saves your data to a hibernation file. Useful for airplanes where devices need to be powered down for a period of time.
