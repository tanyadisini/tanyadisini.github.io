22. Kalimat verbal dan nominal the doctor wirid in the clinic every night


Jawaban:

Kalimat verbal : The doctor wirid in the clinic every night

Kalimat nominal : Doctor's wiridning in the clinic every night.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat Verbal:

Kalimat verbal adalah kalimat yang terdiri dari kata kerja dan biasanya merupakan pernyataan, pertanyaan, atau perintah. Contohnya: Aku berjalan di pantai.

Kalimat Nominal:

Kalimat nominal adalah kalimat yang terdiri dari nama, kata benda, dan biasanya menyatakan suatu fakta. Contohnya: Pantai ini terkenal.

25. 2 mension the characteristic of Clinic thermometer​


Jawaban:

Certainly, here are two characteristics of a clinical thermometer:

1. **Narrow Range:** Clinical thermometers are designed to measure human body temperature, so they typically have a narrow temperature range, often ranging from around 35°C to 42°C (95°F to 108°F). This narrow range allows for precise measurement of body temperature variations.

2. **Mercury-Free:** In many modern clinical thermometers, especially those used in healthcare settings, mercury has been replaced with safer alternatives like alcohol or digital sensors. This change was made to eliminate the health and environmental risks associated with mercury exposure. Digital clinical thermometers, in particular, have become more popular due to their accuracy and ease of use.

These characteristics make clinical thermometers suitable for accurately measuring human body temperature while prioritizing safety and ease of use.
