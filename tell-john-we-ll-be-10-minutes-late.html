19. we ... (be) late if we ... (not lake) now


Jawaban:

We will be late if we don't leave now.

Penjelasan:

The sentence is expressing a future condition and its possible consequence. The verb "will be" is used to indicate a future event or possibility. The word "late" describes the state of being behind schedule or not arriving on time. The phrase "if we don't leave now" introduces the condition that needs to be fulfilled in order to avoid being late. It implies that taking action in the present moment (leaving now) is necessary to prevent the undesirable outcome of being late.

22. We __ (not/be) late again if we __(buy) a new car​


Jawaban:

We would not be late again if we bought a new car​

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran kalimat Conditional Sentence Type 2 (kalimat pengandaian tipe 2) dalam Bahasa inggris.

Kalimat pengandaian tipe 2:

Fakta: Kondisi yang tidak mungkin terwujud di masa kini.

If + subject + V2/simple past tense + Subject + would + Vl/past future tense

atau

If + subject + were + adjective/noun+Subject + would be + adjective/noun

Contoh:

If l were you, I would accept that job offer. Fakta: I am not you.
