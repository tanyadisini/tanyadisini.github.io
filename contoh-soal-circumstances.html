1. Apa yg dimaksud dengan circumstances​


Circumstances is a fact or condition connected with or relevant to be an event or action.

Used in official statements for explaining that something unexpected happened that will prevent an event or situation from continuing normally.

Circumstance sentences example.

• Given the circumstances, she could forgive him.

• I wish the circumstances were different.

• I can't imagine a circumstances in which i would do that.

21. 5. Write the circumstances found in the text! a. Adverbs b. c. Adverbial clause d. Finite and non-finite clauses: Prepositional phrase​


Jawaban:

It appears you're looking for different elements within a text. Let me help you identify the different elements you mentioned:

a. **Adverbs**: Adverbs are words that modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs to provide more information about how, when, where, or to what extent an action is performed. For example, in the sentence "She sang beautifully," "beautifully" is an adverb describing how she sang.

b. **Adverbial clause**: An adverbial clause is a group of words that functions as an adverb in a sentence. It provides information about the time, place, manner, condition, purpose, or reason of an action. For example, in the sentence "He ran because he was late," "because he was late" is an adverbial clause explaining why he ran.

c. **Finite and non-finite clauses**: Clauses are groups of words that contain a subject and a verb.

- **Finite clauses**: These contain a verb in a form that shows tense, person, and number. For example, in the sentence "She is reading a book," "She is reading" is a finite clause.

- **Non-finite clauses**: These contain a verb in a form that does not show tense, person, or number. For example, in the sentence "To read books is my hobby," "To read books" is a non-finite clause.

d. **Prepositional phrase**: A prepositional phrase is a group of words that begins with a preposition and usually ends with a noun or pronoun. It provides additional information about location, time, or manner in a sentence. For example, in the sentence "He sat on the chair," "on the chair" is a prepositional phrase, and "on" is the preposition.

To identify these elements in a specific text, you would need to examine the text and pick out examples that fit each category.

29. 2.Change the clauses about circumstances into non-finite clauses!a. When they saw the police, the thieves ran.b. Because he studied hard, Aditya passes the test.​


REDUCED ADVERBIAL CLAUSE

a. Seeing the police, the thieves ran.

b. Having studied hard, Aditya passes the test.

------------------

EXPLANATION

Adverbial clause adalah adalah anak kalimat yang yang berfungsi sebagai adverb; memodifikasi verb, adjective atau adverb lain.

Adverbial clause terdiri dari subjek dan finite verb.

Adverbial clause dapat dipadatkan menjadi sebuah adverbial phrase dengan syarat subjek adverbial clause tersebut sama dengan subjek induk kalimatnya.

Langkah-langkah mengubah adverbial clause menjadi adverbial phrase:

hilangkan subjekubah finite verb menjadi non-finite verb

Pelajari lebih lanjut tentang finite dan non-finite verbs:

https://brainly.co.id/tugas/23207216

----------------

EXPLICATION

Untuk memadatkan adverbial clause of casuality menjadi adverbial phrase, kita hilangkan kata sambung (when, because, as, dll) dan subjek lalu ubah finite verbnya ke dalam bentuk present participle (v-ing).

Jadi,  

a. When they saw the police ↠ seeing the police

b. Because he studied hard ↠ *having studied hard  

*menggunakan perfect participle (having+past participle) karena menyatakan suatu aksi yang sudah selesai mendahului aksi lain.
