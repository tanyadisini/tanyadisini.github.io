38. Wounds - deep - the - on - cream - do not - use


do not use the cream on the deep woundsBefore we can get the asnwer, we need to find the meaning of each word.
Wounds = LukaDeep = DalamThe = (kata penghubung)On = pada/diatasCream = KrimDo not = janganUse = memakai
From the meaning above, we could arrange the sentence into
Jangan memakai krim pada luka (yang) dalam
Therefore the asnwer is Do not use cream on the deep wounds
