Jawaban :

PEMBAHASAN

Kalimat di atas merupakan contoh kalimat dari conditional sentence type 3. Hal ini diketahui dari penggunaan tenses pada if clause nya :

If you had lockedIf you had reported

Dimana pada if clause nya menggunakan rumus past perfect, sehingga untuk kalimat conditional nya harus menggunakan would + have + V3.

Rumus Past Perfect

S + Had + V3 + O

You had locked your car.You had reported your money to the police.

Tipe Conditional Sentence

Conditional Type 1

If + simple present, conditional (= will study)

If you lock your car, somebody will not steal it.If you report your money to the police, you will not feel guilty.

Conditional Type 2

If + simple past, conditional (=would + V1)

If you locked your car, somebody would not steal it.If you reported your money to the police, you would not feel guilty.

Conditional Type 3

If + past perfect, conditional (=would + have + V3)

If you had locked your car, somebody would not have stolen it.If you had reported your money to the police, you would not have felt guilty.
