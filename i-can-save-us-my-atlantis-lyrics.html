Pembahasan

Verb adalah kata yang mendefinisikan aksi, tindakan dan pengalaman. Yang termasuk verb dalam lirik lagu tersebut adalah Left, pour, feel, lie, get, exchange, broke, said, take back, coming, save, fall, built, pull it down, fled, scared, known, become, reach, could, go, wonder, leave, hurt, love.Adjective adalah kata yang digunakan untuk menerangkan kata benda. Yang termasuk adjective dalam lirik lagu tersebut adalah Hard, high, down, shaky.Noun adalah kata yang digunakan untuk menyebut orang, benda dan tempat. Yang termasuk noun dalam lrik lagu tersebut adalah Bird, tree, light, heart, head, Atlantis, town, ground, love.

when do we use "that"

That : determiner and pronoun

We use that most commonly to point to a thing or person. We use it with singular nouns. The thing or person is often distant from the speaker and sometimes closer to the listener, or not visible to either the speaker or listener:

example 1 : Can you pass me that green bowl over there? (determiner)

[pointing to one of a selection of different paint colours]

example : I quite like that one.

That : relative pronoun

We use that to introduce defining relative clauses. We can use that instead of who, whom or which to refer to people, animals and things. That is more informal than who or which:

example 1 : She picked up the hairbrush that she had left on the bed.

That - clauses  

We also use that to introduce that-clauses after some verbs, adjectives and nouns:

example 1 : I admit that I was wrong. (verb + that-clause)

example 2 : Are you certain that the man in the car was Nick? (adjective + that-clause)

That : other uses

That’s + adjective

We use that’s + adjective (e.g. that’s lovely, that’s good, that’s great, that’s terrible, that’s awful) to respond to something that someone is telling us, to show that we are listening:

example :  A :  They got stuck in traffic on the way to the airport and missed the plane.

B :  Oh, that’s awful.
