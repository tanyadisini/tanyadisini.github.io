The coconut tree

Soal ini terdapat beberapa pertanyaan mengenai teks yang membahan tentang The coconut tree (pohon kelapa). Teks di atas belum lengkap, adapun teks lengkapnya adalah sebagai berikut :

Coconut tree is a useful tropical plant. People called it a coconut palm tree. We can find a lot in Indonesia because Indonesia is a tropical country. It is one of renewable natural resource. It is a tall and hard tree. People can use all parts of this plant. The coconut, its fruits, is usually used for Javanese cooking. It is the material of cooking oil. The dry coconut is called copra. The stem can be used for building material for example frame of doors or windows. The leaves are also useful. The old leaves are for the roof of houses and the young ones are for decoration or the pack of cakes and the ribs are also used for broom. It is not hard to find coconut tree. It can grow everywhere, but it usually grows in the seashore well
