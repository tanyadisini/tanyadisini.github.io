1. A social worker will help other people who want to help.. a. Themselves b. Themself c. Theirself d. Theirs


a. themselves

karena sebagai objek sehingga them dan them lebih dari satu sehinggamenggunakan selves

REFLEXIVE PRONOUNS

A social worker will help other people who want to help themselves.

----------

EXPLANATION

Reflexive pronoun adalah kata ganti berakhiran -self atau -selves yang berfungsi sebagai objek langsung suatu klausa.

Reflexive pronoun digunakan ketika subjek dan objek kalimat sama. Artinya subjek melakukan aksi kepada dirinya sendiri.

Berikut adalah pasangan subjek dengan reflexive pronounnya:

I ⇨ myself

you (kamu) ⇨ yourself

he ⇨ himself

she ⇨ herself

it ⇨ itself

you (kalian) ⇨ yourselves

we ⇨ ourselves

they ⇨ themselves

-------------

EXPLICATION

[...] other people who want to help ...

Subjek klausa di atas adalah other people. Kata ganti untuk other people adalah they, maka reflexive yang digunakan adalah themselves (opsi A).

15. reflexive pronoun dari [they] itu "themselves" "theirselves" itu salah atau boleh digunakan juga??


Since one of these words is never correct, it’s easy to remember themselves vs. theirselves.

Themselves is the correct word.

Theirselves is nonstandard.

A way to remember this is that their is possessive. Since reflexive pronouns are not used to indicate possession, it should be easy to remember that themselves is the correct form of this word.
